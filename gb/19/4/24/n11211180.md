<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2>“奇观2019”加拿大王室马戏团将在加拿大巡演</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Royal-Canadian-Circus-Canada-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加拿大王室马戏团（Royal Canadian Family Circus）“奇观2019”将在大温、阿尔伯塔省和安省巡演。图为一演出场地
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月25日讯】享誉盛名的<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E7%8E%8B%E5%AE%A4%E9%A9%AC%E6%88%8F%E5%9B%A2.md">加拿大王室马戏团</a>（<a href="https://www.royalcanadiancircus.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Royal Canadian Family Circus</a>）“<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E8%A7%822019.md">奇观2019</a>”（<a href="https://www.royalcanadiancircus.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">SPECTAC!2019</a>）将从5月9日～8月18日在<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md">卑诗</a>省、阿尔伯塔省和安省的10个城市巡演，总计演出108场；带来一套“全新的、充满了爆炸性的节目。”</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E7%8E%8B%E5%AE%A4%E9%A9%AC%E6%88%8F%E5%9B%A2.md">加拿大王室马戏团</a>拥有52年的历史。“<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E8%A7%822019.md">奇观2019</a>”将捕捉你童年时代的所有马戏团的怀旧情绪，让你感受现代的曲折和迷人的刺激。在大顶之下，看着惊人的飞行家和惊险的空中飞人，敏捷的杂技演员和令人眼花缭乱的杂耍者，你将感到惊心动魄、心旷神怡、和流连忘返！</p>
<p><a width="640" b="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Rv6uCQrXx8o?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></a></p>
<p>“<a href="https://www.royalcanadiancircus.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">奇观2019</a>”将为观众奉献传统及现代马戏节目，如惊险的命运之轮、高空秋千、中国杂技表演、空中摩托车特技等，非常适合全家老少观看。</p>
<p>加拿大王室马戏团由著名的马戏团班主约瑟夫?多米尼克?鲍尔（Joseph Dominik Bauer）领队，汇集了全球身怀绝技的顶级马戏演员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11211218" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Joseph-Dominik-Bauer.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11211218 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Joseph-Dominik-Bauer-450x675.jpg" alt="加拿大王室马戏团班主Joseph Dominik Bauer" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加拿大王室马戏团班主Joseph Dominik Bauer</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自马戏表演世家，至今已是第九代马戏世家的约瑟夫?鲍尔在马戏表演界享有盛望。他还将表演他的拿手好戏――高难度、险象环生的命运之轮(Wheel of Destiny)，令人叹为观止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11211222" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Wheel-of-Destiny.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11211222" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Wheel-of-Destiny-450x338.jpg" alt="命运之轮" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">约瑟夫?鲍尔的拿手好戏命运之轮(Wheel of Destiny)</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自南美哥伦比亚的空中杂技绝活(THE FLYING CORTES FAMILY)，由Robinson Cortes，他的妻子和家人，在空中精心运作、难以置信的杂技双人、三人和四人动作。著名的Cortes一家曾在世界各大马戏团中演出，获得了国际马戏团奖，曾多次在国家电视台播出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11211224" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/THE-FLYING-CORTES-FAMILY.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11211224" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/THE-FLYING-CORTES-FAMILY-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">来自南美哥伦比亚的空中杂技绝活(THE FLYING CORTES FAMILY)</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国杂技团将给观众表演高难的中国传统的平衡、杂技等。他们超越极限的椅子平衡、花瓶平衡，扭曲、空竹等特技将给观众带来难忘的记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11211227" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Chairs-balance.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11211227" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Chairs-balance-450x560.jpg" alt="椅子平衡" width="450" b="560" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中国传统杂技椅子平衡</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11211229" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Bottle-balance.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11211229" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Bottle-balance.jpg" alt="花瓶平衡" width="350" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中国传统杂技花瓶平衡</figcaption></figure>
<p>您还将欣赏独特的平衡表演(The ROLA BOLA ARISTOV)。来自俄罗斯的乔治?阿里斯托夫在悬浮在空中时执行各种手部平衡运动技能，令人难以置信的旋转和旋转错觉是通过快速和激烈的精确手眼协调来完成的！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11211231" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/The-ROLA-BOLA-ARISTOV.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11211231" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/The-ROLA-BOLA-ARISTOV.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">平衡表演(The ROLA BOLA ARISTOV)</figcaption></figure>
<p>您还将见证激动人心的多明格斯摩托车环球(XTREME DOMINGUEZ Family Motorcycle Globe)的技能和勇敢的壮举。这些飞速、勇敢的表演，没有丝毫瑕疵的余地，来自哥伦比亚的第8代马戏世家继续将其演出推向极限。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11211232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/XTREME-DOMINGUEZ-Family-Motorcycle-Globe.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11211232" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/XTREME-DOMINGUEZ-Family-Motorcycle-Globe-450x360.jpg" alt="摩托车环球" width="450" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">多明格斯摩托车环球(XTREME DOMINGUEZ Family Motorcycle Globe)</figcaption></figure>
<p>精彩马戏的普通票只有25元。《大纪元》读者还享有送一特别的优惠(网购折扣码为epoch)。请访问在线购票网页：<a href="https://www.royalcanadiancircus.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">www.royalcanadiancircus.ca</a>。</p>
