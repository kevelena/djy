<h1>多伦多台商会主办“2019台湾美食国际巡讲”</h1>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/e2b6e3a089080eefe988bac040cd1135-600x400.jpg" />


<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月26日讯】<span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="ZH-TW">由<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md">多伦多台商会</a>主办，中华民国侨务委员会指导，将在</span>2019<span lang="ZH-TW">年</span>5<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>18<span lang="ZH-TW">日</span><span lang="ZH-TW">晚</span><span lang="ZH-TW">于</span><span lang="ZH-TW">绿的小窝（</span>Green Grotto）<span lang="ZH-TW">举行“</span>2019<span lang="ZH-TW">年<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md">台湾美食</a>国际巡回讲座”。</span></span></p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md">多伦多台商会</a>主办此活动的目的是希望透过名厨现场示范烹调诀窍，让华人或其他族裔的消费者，学到如何巧妙运用食材，在家轻松烹煮道地的<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md">台湾美食</a>。</p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">本年讲座特别邀请来自台湾，多次荣获国、内外厨艺竞赛奖项，并</span><span lang="ZH-TW">曾任北台湾科技学院台菜兼任讲师、景文科技大学日本料理兼任讲师、桃园县政府达人希望学校讲师，延益健康食研所所长潘盟仁</span><span lang="ZH-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">潘盟仁讲师</span><span lang="ZH-TW">将于</span><span lang="ZH-TW">绿的小窝</span><span lang="ZH-TW">亲自传授台湾料理窍门，包括台湾牛肉面等</span>5~6<span lang="ZH-TW">道知名的台湾料理。待厨艺教学后，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">现场民众可享受到美味的示范菜料理及牛肉面套餐</span>，<span lang="ZH-TW">附送饮料</span>1<span lang="ZH-TW">杯，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">同时也开放</span>Q&amp;A<span lang="ZH-TW">供询问</span><span lang="ZH-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">报名请致电多伦多台商会秘书处</span><span lang="EN-CA">，电话（416）439-9778，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">或</span><span lang="EN-CA">?email至<a href="https://mail.epochtimes.com/cgi-bin/webmail/login/canada_editor@epochtimes.com.authvchkpw/7F16FCE8E39CC13578E4F7F689FF520F/1556220519?folder=INBOX&amp;form=newmsg&amp;to=testt@rogers.com" rel="nofollow">testt@rogers.com</a></span></p>
