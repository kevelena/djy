<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2>BTS于BBMAs摘两冠 归功与ARMY分享微小事物</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010325100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>防弹少年团（BTS）于2019 Billboard Music Awards摘得两奖。(Amy Sussman/Getty Images for dcp)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月02日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）韩国人气男团<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md">防弹少年团</a>（BTS），今（2）日在2019告示牌音乐奖（Billboard Music Awards，简称BBMAs）拿下最佳社群艺人（Top Social Artist）与最佳团体（Top Duo／Group）两个奖项。他们因此成为首个于告示牌音乐奖摘下最佳团体且摘下两个奖项的韩国歌手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11228461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010307100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11228461" title="BTS" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010307100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md">防弹少年团</a>（BTS）出席2019 BBMAs红毯活动仍是开心的跳跃着拍照。(Frazer Harrison/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11228457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010255100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11228457" title="BTS" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010255100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）出席2019 Billboard Music Awards红毯照。 (Emma McIntyre/Getty Images for dcp)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在走红毯接受采访时，队长RM（金南俊）谈及，这次新歌的合作对象Halsey（海尔希），其实是两年前参与告示牌音乐奖期间认识的，由于是彼此的粉丝，Halsey前往韩国才能够促成了这次的合作，为此能够有这样的合作，其实是在告示牌音乐奖结下的缘分。他们也在这次的颁奖典礼中与Halsey共同演出了合作曲《Boy With Luv》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11228449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010404100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11228449" title="BTS and Halsey" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010404100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS and Halsey" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团于告示牌音乐奖与Halsey共同演出《Boy With Luv》。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images for dcp)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11228454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010348100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11228454" title="BTS" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010348100707-600x399.jpg" alt="BTS" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团于2019 Billboard Music Awards舞台演出照。(Ethan Miller/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>自2017年起已连续两年摘得“最佳社群艺人（Top Social Artist）”的防弹少年团，本次与韩国人气团体EXO、GOT7，以及亚莉安娜（Ariana Grande）和路易?汤姆林森（Louis Tomlinson）角逐该奖，并成功达成三连霸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11228529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190501232734100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11228529" title="BTS poses in the press room with their awards during the 2019 Billboard Music Awards" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190501232734100707-600x399.jpg" alt="BTS poses in the press room with their awards during the 2019 Billboard Music Awards" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于2019 Billboard Music Awards摘得两奖。 (BRIDGET BENNETT/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>防弹少年团在本次的告示牌音乐奖中，还与魔力红（Maroon 5）、谜幻乐团（Imagine Dragons）、丹和沙伊（Dan + Shay）、乐团迪斯可瘪三（Panic! At the Disco）共同角逐“最佳团体（Top Duo／Group）”奖，首度入围该奖的他们，本次也成功拿下了这个受到各界瞩目的奖项。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11228444" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010321100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11228444" title="BTS accept the Top Duo/Group award onstage during the 2019 Billboard Music Awards" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/190502010321100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS accept the Top Duo/Group award onstage during the 2019 Billboard Music Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团于2019 BBMAs摘得最佳团体奖，由RM发表得奖感言。(Ethan Miller/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在主持人宣布BTS获奖时，成员们都难掩惊讶的张大了嘴、开心的鼓掌，J-hope（郑号锡）与<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%BE%E5%9B%BD.md">凸</a>更是开心的接连跳了起来，台下的粉丝们则是齐声欢呼，还有粉丝在以手机记录这一刻时，激动的掩面哭泣。</p>
<p>队长RM在众人的欢呼中发表得奖感言。他大声呼喊：“Thank you ARMY（官方粉丝名）！仍然不敢相信我们能够站在这个舞台、与这么多了不起的歌手们在一起，我的天哪！这是因为我们分享着各种微小事物才能够办到的，这就是BTS和ARMY的力量，对吧？”</p>
