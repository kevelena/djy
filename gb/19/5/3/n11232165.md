<h1>图说英国一周：伦敦马拉松</h1>



<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月03日讯】一年一度的<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A6%E6%95%A6%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md">伦敦马拉松</a>在4月28日举行，今年有超过44.5万人申请，人数创历史最高，最终有超过5.6万人参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11232181" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/1-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11232181 size-full" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/1-3.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="447" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在终点的参赛者和志愿者。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11232190" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/2-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232190" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/2-2.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="330" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在首先举行的专业组比赛中，肯尼亚选手包揽男子组和女子组冠军。（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>长跑名将吉普乔格（Eliud Kipchoge）创下了这一赛事的新纪录，这也是他第四次赢得<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A6%E6%95%A6%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md">伦敦马拉松</a>的冠军。<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD.md">英国</a>选手法赫获得第五名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11232196" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/UK909_B_03_In-photo_Qian.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232196" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/UK909_B_03_In-photo_Qian.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="386" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD.md">英国</a>运动员Hayley Carruthers。（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>英国运动员Hayley Carruthers在长跑过程中用尽全力，到了距离终点只差一米的地方腿部脱力，跌倒在地。但是她没有放弃，爬过了终点，最终以2小时33分59秒的时间完成比赛，这是她个人最好成绩。</p>
<p>她后来接受采访的时候表示，到了接近终点的时候，“我开始数着自己的步伐，我的意识消失了，我想我有一只眼睛看不见了。我抬不起腿了，不能动了”。</p>
<p>她的本职工作是国民保健服务研究癌症的放射科医生，第二天就正常上班了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11232202" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232202" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="649" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">男超人。（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11232208" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/4-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232208" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/4-1.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="608" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女超人。（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11232211" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232211" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/5.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="699" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“大本钟”先生。（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>业余组比赛中许多参赛者都身穿奇装异服，包括男超人、女超人、蝙蝠侠、蜘蛛人、帕丁顿熊。还有这位“大本钟”先生，可惜钟的高度太高，到了终点的时候被卡住，只好由工作人员帮助他弯下腰，才完成任务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11232217" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/6.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232217" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/6.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="459" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>伦敦马拉松也是人们为慈善机构筹款的好机会。图中的这位“太阳男”为“患癌症的儿童”（Children With Cancer）筹到了超过6,000镑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11232220" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/7.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11232220" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/7.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="737" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的比赛还有来自台湾的视障选手39岁的洪国展参加。他在陪跑员杨钟鼎的帮助下完成全程，为英国皇家盲人协会（RNIB）筹集到了2,000镑的捐款。图中的选手为一名来自台湾的参赛者在终点前做最后的冲刺。◇</p>
