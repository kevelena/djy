<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>出国放松又不失时尚　日系慵懒系穿搭</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/c4c3a0d34556c0cccfc683c259eb9947-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>可当洋装穿的长版衬衫。（业者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月27日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲报导）近期<a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E7%B3%BB.md">日系</a><a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md">穿搭</a>相当流行慵懒风，不只轻便自在，而且能让<a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A6%E5%81%87.md">度假</a>造型变得<a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md">时尚</a>，受到众多年轻女性喜爱，只要利用以下四种单品就能打造出慵懒的度假时尚感。</p>
<p>编织遮阳帽：想要美美的享受阳光又怕晒黑、想穿<a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md">时尚</a>泳装又不想包得像粽子，记得戴上遮阳帽，透气又轻巧，更能搭出时尚感。</p>
<figure id="11282375" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/c221470ab93ff91d98cb2f208e92632b-450x450.png" alt="草编遮阳帽。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">草编遮阳帽。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>长款开襟衬衫：日剧女主角身穿长款衬衫，一衣两穿很受欢迎，全扣起来可当洋装，塑造优雅又慵懒的氛围；以开襟衬衫方式搭配简约休闲素色棒球帽，则能带出休闲感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11282372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/0cc116a547cbf9aabb3469c072b8c1d9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11282372" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/0cc116a547cbf9aabb3469c072b8c1d9-600x1112.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1112" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">可当洋装穿的长版衬衫。 （业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高腰宽版牛仔裤：宽松穿着是慵懒时尚风的要素，若以高腰宽版牛仔裤作为<a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md">穿搭</a>主角，搭配简约宽敞短袖上衣，出国活动相当舒适；若上衣挑选贴身的罗纹针织短袖上衣，则能穿出上窄下宽的平衡视觉效果。</p>
<figure id="11282373" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/a741956f4aac737d65bdf1a641ed98ac-450x350.jpg" alt="高腰牛仔宽?裤。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">高腰牛仔宽?裤。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>皱折宽版长裤：宽版长裤穿起来时尚大方，上身套上防紫外线简约衬衫，呈现出满满<a href="https://github.com/szzd3/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E7%B3%BB.md">日系</a>慵懒风气息。</p>
<figure id="11282374" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9b4722fb0158ac1c6f19ff2c8a4abe16-450x450.jpg" alt="舒适宽?裤。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">舒适宽?裤。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
