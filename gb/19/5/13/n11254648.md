<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2>阿富汗男孩“与义肢共舞” 笑容感染全球网友</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/amputee-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>阿富汗喀布尔红十字会骨科中心物理治疗师穆卡拉?拉希米（Mulkara Rahimi）（右），为五岁男孩艾哈迈德?拉曼（Ahmad Rahman）（左）装上义肢，男孩的人生从此不一样了。（<a target="_blank" href="http://mulkararahimi.com/" target="_blank">MulkaraRahimi.com
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年05月15日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏综合报导）对失去下肢的人来说，如能戴上<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E8%82%A2.md">义肢</a>从新行走，感觉一定有如重生。近日一则阿富汗红十字会的感人影片就是最好的见证。</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%AF%8C%E6%B1%97%E5%86%85%E6%88%98.md">阿富汗内战</a>不断，政府军和塔利班的冲突使得烽火连年。几年前，洛加尔省村庄里一对姊弟误触地雷，只有8个月大的弟弟艾哈迈德?拉曼（Ahmad Rahman）和姊姊萨莉玛都受重伤。弟弟的右腿被炸断，被迫截肢。</p>
<p>后来小男孩装了<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E8%82%A2.md">义肢</a>，今年5岁的他，一直在适应人工右腿。最近，个头长高的他在更换新义肢后，开心地绕圈子跳起民族舞。激励人心的一幕，让网友们看了都为他高兴，许多人也感动分享这则“与义肢共舞”影片。</p>
<p>拍下影片的是喀布尔（Kabul）红十字会骨科中心的理疗师穆卡拉?拉希米（<a href="http://mulkararahimi.com/about/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mulkara Rahimi</a>），她负责为男孩安装义肢。这已经是拉曼的第4副义肢了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11260719" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Helping-Children-with-Disabilities-in-Afghanistan.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11260719 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Helping-Children-with-Disabilities-in-Afghanistan-450x600.jpg" alt="阿富汗喀布尔（Kabul）红十字会骨科中心（International Committee of the Red Cross）的物理治疗师穆卡拉?拉希米（Mulkara Rahimi）（右），为五岁男孩艾哈迈德?拉曼（Ahmad Rahman）装上义肢。" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">理疗师穆卡拉?拉希米（Mulkara Rahimi）（右），为五岁男孩艾哈迈德?拉曼（Ahmad Rahman，左）装上义肢。(<a href="http://mulkararahimi.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MulkaraRahimi.com</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>装上义肢的拉曼，行动越来越自如了，轻松到能跳舞，他露出天真无邪又满足的笑容，满脸写着骄傲和成就感。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">相关影片?</span></strong></p>
<p><center><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pKvYKM5_dLQ?" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>拉希米说，拉曼开心的笑容感染很多人，给工作人员带来极大的鼓励，所以才拍下影片传看。经红十字会员工罗亚?穆萨维（Roya Musawi）在推特分享后，立即引来广大回响。网友们感染了男孩的开心，称赞他“好可爱”、“笑容好灿烂”、“像小天使”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11261067" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/GettyImages-1141913658.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11261067 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/GettyImages-1141913658-600x381.jpg" alt="Ahmad Sayed Rahman, a five-year-old Afghan boy who lost his right leg when he was hit by a bullet in the crossfire of a battle, walks without his prosthetic leg at the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) hospital for war victims and the disabled, in Kabul on May 7, 2019. - With his hands in the air and an infectious grin spreading from ear to ear, a young Afghan boy whirls around a Kabul hospital room on his new prosthetic leg. <p>事实上，拉曼的遭遇并不是唯一，而是一个很普遍的现象。阿富汗战火造成当地数不清的无辜百姓的死伤，像他这样的伤患可能有上万例，许多伤残者失去手臂或双腿。红十字会主任艾伯托?开罗（Alberto Cairo）向《华盛顿邮报》表示，这则影片的广传，引起了人们的重视和关切。</p>
<p>影片目前已获得几百万次播放，这让拉希米受到很大的鼓舞，她希望能有更多人知道阿富汗人民的现况。她在个人网站“<a href="http://MulkaraRahimi.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MulkaraRahimi.com</a>”上写道，“拉曼只是千分之一而已，还有更多像这样的孩童伤残故事，我想告诉更多人。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11261068" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/GettyImages-1141913675.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11261068 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/GettyImages-1141913675-450x670.jpg" alt="Ahmad Sayed Rahman, a five-year-old Afghan boy who lost his right leg when he was hit by a bullet in the crossfire of a battle, sits on a bench without his prosthetic leg at the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) hospital for war victims and the disabled, in Kabul on May 7, 2019. - With his hands in the air and an infectious grin spreading from ear to ear, a young Afghan boy whirls around a Kabul hospital room on his new prosthetic leg. <p>拉希米自己也是伤残者，靠义肢行动的她曾接受红十字会的治疗，她的亲身经历使得理疗工作更加得心应手，因为她完全明白病人的需要和适应过程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11261045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/57277826_2139767392810321_5291920615190560768_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11261045 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/57277826_2139767392810321_5291920615190560768_n-600x400.jpg" alt="物理治疗师穆卡拉?拉希米（Mulkara Rahimi）经常参加残障体育竞赛。(&lt;a href=&quot;http://mulkararahimi.com/about/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;MulkaraRahimi.com&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">理疗师穆卡拉?拉希米（Mulkara Rahimi，中）经常参加<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AE%8B%E9%9A%9C.md">残障</a>体育竞赛。(<a href="http://mulkararahimi.com/about/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MulkaraRahimi.com</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>40年来的战火，使得超过百万的阿富汗人民变成伤残人。根据红十字会统计，自1988年以来，阿富汗有46,100人截肢、178,000人有不同的身体残疾，骨科中心每天都有数百人来安装义肢。红十字会历年的伤残病患中，有三分之二是由于误触地雷或遭流弹波及。</p>
