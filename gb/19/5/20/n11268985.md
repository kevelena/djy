<h6>真正聪明的人，回看生命时应是微笑、淡然的，一生的经历足以让他们想清楚了其中的因果，找到到底是什么在主导命运的答案。(fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>向前行进<br />
向前行进……</p>
<p>已经向前行进多远了<br />
还是要向前行进</p>
<p>已经向前行进多久了<br />
还是要向前行进</p>
<p>辗过多少崎岖坎坷了<br />
还是要向前行进</p>
<p>历经多少风尘雪雨了<br />
还是要向前行进</p>
<p>皮肤都已皲裂了<br />
还是要向前行进</p>
<p>向前行进<br />
就是向前行进<br />
不去标榜什么任重道远那些伟大的口号……</p>
<p><strong> The Wheel</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>March forward<br />
March forward…</p>
<p>How far has it marched forward<br />
March forward still</p>
<p>How long has it marched forward<br />
March forward still</p>
<p>How many ruggedness it has pressed<br />
Mach forward still</p>
<p>Experienced how many wind, dust rain and snow<br />
March forward still…</p>
<p>It’s chapped of the skin<br />
March forward still</p>
<p>March forward<br />
Is marched forward<br />
Not to flaunt what so call those great slogan@</p>
