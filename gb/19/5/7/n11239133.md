<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月07日讯】（大纪元记者青羽综合报导）<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A4%E7%81%AB%E8%99%AB.md">萤火虫</a>是昆虫界的萤光小天使，当它们约定好一起在森林的夜空里翩然起舞时，那种萤光闪闪的画面，让人心里由然生出满满的疗愈感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11239144" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/7273e41660d42912b8e86c091bcc766d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239144" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/7273e41660d42912b8e86c091bcc766d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">飞舞在夜空中的萤光小天使们。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11239137" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ce224a7a122922f4ffe91cce3b1c7775.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239137" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ce224a7a122922f4ffe91cce3b1c7775-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">萤光闪闪的画面，充满疗愈感。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11239150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/2c7bc989f74d00db6269b8751ecce7be.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239150" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/2c7bc989f74d00db6269b8751ecce7be-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">飞旋的<a href="https://github.com/szzd1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A4%E7%81%AB%E8%99%AB.md">萤火虫</a>，是森林的光之精灵。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11239138" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/22cea9e75014af071b18e399f5cfdeed.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239138" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/22cea9e75014af071b18e399f5cfdeed-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">飞旋的萤火虫，是森林的光之精灵。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着社会进步，萤火虫也越来越难见到。因此在赏萤火虫时，常有家长带着小朋友来开眼界，当小朋友们看着飞旋的萤火虫在身边飞舞而发出的赞叹声时，让赏萤更添趣味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11239151" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/cc9ce66810f8243091999a7472f671d9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239151" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/cc9ce66810f8243091999a7472f671d9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在空中飞舞的多是雄虫，雌虫则多栖息于草丛里。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11239139" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/dc1de83c36b5c363a8a458cb79b46f7d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239139" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/dc1de83c36b5c363a8a458cb79b46f7d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在空中飞舞的多是雄虫，雌虫则多栖息于草丛里。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萤火虫的幼虫期约十个月，会捕食小蜗牛、蚯蚓、蛞蝓、螺贝类。破蛹而出后的成虫，因口器退化只能喝露水，并且只有20天左右的生命以完成传宗接代的任务。通常在空中飞舞的多是雄虫，雌虫则多栖息于草丛里。</p>
<p>每年4到6月是台湾各地萤火虫数量最多的时候。如阳明山天溪园、乌来、新店和美山、安坑牛伯伯生态园区、土城桐花公园、承天禅寺、龙泉路、善息堂、青云路、三峡鸢尾山长春园、皇后镇森林、大板根、汐止汐碇路、新竹内湾东窝溪、北埔冷泉、台中东势林场、云林古坑大华山区、南投奥万大、日月潭、阿里山、台南梅岭、屏东垦丁社顶自然公园等，都是适合赏萤的地点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11239142" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/5701947d499e3a71b1fdd0635cc1b034.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239142" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/5701947d499e3a71b1fdd0635cc1b034-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">光害少、有水潭、溪流、沟渠、有蛙鸣与猛禽盘旋等处，较有机会发现萤火虫。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11239140" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ee7c54f0553aa4b4193671f0309de3e8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239140" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ee7c54f0553aa4b4193671f0309de3e8-450x305.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">路灯照不到的黑暗草丛处，是萤火虫容易出现的地方。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11239152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/c67c175caa8e6dc1298ecb383016479a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239152" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/c67c175caa8e6dc1298ecb383016479a-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">路灯照不到的黑暗草丛处，是萤火虫容易出现的地方。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外山区符合光害少、有水潭、溪流、沟渠、晚上有蛙鸣、白天有猛禽盘旋等条件的地方，也有机会发现萤火虫。在路灯照不到的黑暗草丛处，是萤火虫容易出现的地方，不要错过了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11239141" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/08439d6e4e18586baeea12c9feb1c816.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11239141" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/08439d6e4e18586baeea12c9feb1c816-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">晚上6点半到8点左右是萤火虫量最大的时候。（青羽／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚上6点半到8点左右是萤火虫量最大的时候。8点后随着配对成功，萤火虫数量也越来越少。风大、月圆或下雨时，萤火虫现身的数量也会变少。</p>
<p>为了让我们的子孙能够一直有萤火虫可看，赏萤注意事项需要大家共同遵守：</p>
<p>1.应使用红光或包上红色玻璃纸的手电筒，以免影响萤火虫。并且不可直接照射萤火虫。</p>
<p>2.不要捕捉萤火虫，易惊吓而死，应让它们继续在森林里发光。特别是用手捕捉萤火虫后，因为沾染了人的气味，会害它遭到同类的排斥。</p>
<p>3.避免大声喧哗，干扰到萤火虫活动。</p>
<p>4.生态好的地方易有蛇，需穿长裤厚鞋。走路时须打草惊蛇，并照亮地面及垂下的树枝，以避开蛇类，也不要随意走入草丛中。</p>
<p>5.野外蚊虫多，应穿长袖长裤或喷防蚊液。◇</p>
