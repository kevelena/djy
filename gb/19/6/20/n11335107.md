<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《摆谱》料理实做：玛格利特风味猪肉</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/70-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>玛格利特风味猪肉。（图／圆神出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>一个人在家偶尔想开伙，但不想弄得灰头土脸、全身油烟味。此时，一张料理纸，一部小烤箱，简单食材就能变换出美味异国料理。</p>
<p>看图照个摆，就算零厨艺也能轻易变身大厨的料理纸食谱：《摆谱》，让我在家也能享受异国风味。</p>
<p><strong>《摆谱》料理实做：<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E6%A0%BC%E5%88%A9%E7%89%B9%E9%A3%8E%E5%91%B3%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89.md">玛格利特风味猪肉</a></strong></p>
<p>模仿<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md">披萨</a>打造出意大利风味的纸包料理。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>材料很简单</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_11335178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/10-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11335178" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/10-3-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／圆神出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>? 碎猪肉〔100g〕→ 先与 2 撮盐及少许胡椒拌好<br />
? 番茄〔中型1/2个〕→ 切丁<br />
? <a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md">披萨</a>用起司〔30g〕<br />
? 橄榄油 〔1大匙〕<br />
? 罗勒叶〔1-2片〕</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>方法很简单</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_11335181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11335181" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20-1-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／圆神出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11335182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/30-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11335182" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/30-3-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／圆神出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11335183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/40-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11335183" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/40-2-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／圆神出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11335184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/50-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11335184" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/50-1-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／圆神出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>铺摆烤，简单三步骤</strong><br />
1. 准备好材料，依照食谱上指示的内容，进行前置工作。将《摆谱》页面确实打开，准备好比页面稍大张的料理纸，平铺在书上。<br />
2. 从半透明的料理纸看下去，能够看见书上的图案，除了罗勒叶，其它的材料请按照图谱排在料理纸。摆好材料之后，将底下的书本抽走。稳稳地包起来并轻轻摇晃。<br />
3.烤箱事先预热210度，烘烤约15分钟即可完成。盛装在盘上之后，再用手撕碎罗勒叶，撒在料理上。</p>
<p><strong>料理Tips</strong><br />
? 若不使用烤箱，也可以用微波炉加热３分钟左右。注意要让猪肉熟透。</p>
<p><strong>享用很简单</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11335185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/60-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11335185" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/60-1-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／圆神出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>? 出炉后亦可连同料理纸直接上桌，减少餐盘油腻的清洗困扰。<br />
? 料理纸完整包覆食材风味，美味一点也不流失。<br />
? 《摆谱》共收录37道异国风味料理食谱，利用料理纸，可同时准备数道料理，朋友来访也能轻松招待。</p>
