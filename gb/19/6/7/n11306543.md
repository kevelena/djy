<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>1款针织外套 穿出最有感的6种时尚穿搭术</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_33_124-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本女孩认证最有感的时尚穿搭扩充术。（图/时报出版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>穿搭是一种生活态度，是每日视觉艺术，是重视细节的影响力，是挑战创意的日常练习！想要营造<a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md">时尚</a>氛围，穿法上的变化、颜色搭配、配件、发型、体态与姿势等各种要素所构成的“整体比例”，会比“穿什么”更重要，换言之，关键在于“该怎么穿”。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;">基本的<a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%88%E7%BB%87.md">针织</a>外套</span></h3>
<p><a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%88%E7%BB%87.md">针织</a>外套的感觉会随颜色、版型、毛线的粗细及材质而变。她可以当外套、当内搭，甚至当披肩或系在腰上，百搭的实穿性是针织外套的魅力所在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304651" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_62_111.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11304651" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_62_111-450x549.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="549" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">颜色</span></strong>-针织外套披在肩上或系在腰上时，可以当成点缀色，连这么鲜艳的颜色都能使用，是针织外套最大的优点之一。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">V领</span></strong>&#8211; V 领可以酝酿休闲轻松的氛围。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">圆领</span></strong>-圆领能增添女人味，打造温柔可人的形象。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">版型</span></strong>-不要太宽松或太贴身，挑选里面还可加一件薄衫的宽松度最佳。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;">装饰针织外套的领口</span></h3>
<p>针织衫领口的各种变化。搭配经典饰品，既自然又<a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md">时尚</a>。除了这些搭配，也可以自由改造出属于自己的风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304657" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-1_112.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11304657 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-1_112-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加上珍珠项链，优雅又迷人。裤子或裙子建议穿随性、休闲一点来平衡。（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11304658" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-2_113.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11304658" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-2_113-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">内搭衬衫，但领口刻意不露出来。属于较男性化的打扮。（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11304661" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-3_114.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11304661" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-3_114-450x350.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">系上丝巾增添淑女韵味。（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11304662" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-4_115.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11304662" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-4_115-450x350.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">内搭衬衫率性地解开扣子，帅气又有女人味。（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>毛线的粗细与织纹的密度也会改变印象！</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">1. 粗针织衫</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">?</span></strong><span style="color: #993300;"><span style="color: #000000;">毛线较粗、密度偏松、料子较厚。给人随性、休闲、轻松的印象。?</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11304669" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a style="color: #000000;" href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-5_116.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11304669" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-5_116-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><span style="color: #000000;">粗针织衫（毛线较粗、密度偏松、料子较厚。给人随性、休闲、轻松的印象。）（图/时报出版提供）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">2.细针织衫</span></strong></p>
<p>毛线较细、织纹紧密、料子偏薄。给人优雅、高贵的印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304672" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-6_117.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11304672" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_63-6_117-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">细针织衫（毛线较细、织纹紧密、料子偏薄。给人优雅、高贵的印象。）（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">3.以简约打扮为基础，加入一件流行单品</span></strong></p>
<p>以基础色统一的简约打扮。配上流行的水桶包，凸显自我风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304697" style="width: 382px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_64-1_118.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11304697" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_64-1_118.jpg" alt="" width="382" b="690" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">4.披上粗针织的V 领外套，打造慵懒女人味</span></strong></p>
<p>色彩鲜艳的包包，为经典的深蓝色打扮带来新鲜感。想衬托某个颜色时，其他颜色不妨简单一点，比较容易搭配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304718" style="width: 307px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_64-2_119.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11304718" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_64-2_119.jpg" alt="" width="307" b="696" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">5.深色的大衣与长裤用粉红色针织外套当作点缀</span></strong></p>
<p>万用的针织外套还可以内搭当点缀色。里头配了衬衫，休闲又不失正式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304721" style="width: 319px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_65-1_120.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11304721" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_65-1_120.jpg" alt="" width="319" b="702" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">6.工作裤搭灰色针织外套，营造温柔沉稳印象</span></strong></p>
<p>想将朴实的工作裤穿出女人味，配高跟鞋最合适。我很喜欢这种帅气中透着女人味的打扮。用灰色点缀，产生一致性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304724" style="width: 332px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_65-2_121.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11304724" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_65-2_121.jpg" alt="" width="332" b="630" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
