<h2><a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E7%90%83%E8%8A%B1.md">火球花</a>Haemanthus multiflorus<a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E8%92%9C%E7%A7%91.md">石蒜科</a></h2>
<p>又称火球花，红绣球、血百合、<a href="https://github.com/dfh1/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%A3%E7%90%83%E8%8A%B1.md">绣球花</a>、网球花、网球石蒜、虎耳兰。多年生草本，扁球形地下鳞茎，直径约 5～10公分。叶自鳞茎上生出，叶柄基部呈鞘状。5～6月间开花，花茎直立，高 30～70 公分。通常开花时不长叶，谢后新叶才长出。伞形花序由 30～100朵小花组成，呈圆球形；小花红色，有短花筒及 6 瓣细线形花瓣；雄蕊突出花冠向外，呈火红色，有明显黄色花药。浆果成熟时红色，结果率不高。盆栽或园植均很理想。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11320554" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/c0aeaf183f2d317771554dd581b47e1d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11320554" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/c0aeaf183f2d317771554dd581b47e1d-450x431.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="431" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">盆栽或园植均很理想。叶及鳞茎辛、凉，有小毒。（摄影／郑清海）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11320556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/c9bd655939ad9e0e26564a25926a3877.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11320556" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/c9bd655939ad9e0e26564a25926a3877-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鳞茎含有毒的植物碱。可用播种或分株（分球）法繁殖。（摄影／郑清海）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11320555" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9607db0ac26a5774d7164870c9916069.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11320555" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9607db0ac26a5774d7164870c9916069-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">叶自鳞茎上生出，叶柄基部呈鞘状。叶及鳞茎辛、凉，有小毒。鳞茎能解毒，散瘀，消肿。外用治无名肿毒。（摄影／郑清海）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民国47年才引进台湾，目前各地种植相当普遍。鳞茎含有毒的植物碱。可用播种或分株（分球）法繁殖。花谢后能结红色浆果种子，用种子播种需经 5年以上培育才能开花。一般均采用分株、分球法，经 1～2年即能开花。盆栽或园植均很理想。叶及鳞茎辛、凉，有小毒。鳞茎能解毒，散瘀，消肿。外用治无名肿毒。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11320558" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/be9548a73bfc7f5a2495cd39aff5753e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11320558" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/be9548a73bfc7f5a2495cd39aff5753e-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一般均采用分株、分球法，经1～2年即能开花。盆栽或园植均很理想。（摄影／郑清海）</figcaption></figure>
