<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台中最高咖啡馆 几百台币乐享美食景观</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/27072279_1032494323558727_5349031236364406117_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>位于台湾台中市36楼高层的咖啡任务餐厅，是台中市最高的高空景观餐厅。在此就餐，高空美景一览无余。（<a target="_blank"="https://www.facebook.com/CafeTask/photos/a.961115230696637/1032494323558727/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CafeTask 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年06月04日讯】（大纪元记者李潇综合报导）在高耸入云的<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E8%A7%82%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md">景观餐厅</a>，一边品味香醇浓郁的咖啡与精心烘培的各色糕点，一边欣赏窗外的旖旎风光，与知己好友倾谈，这样赏心悦目的欧美风<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1%E9%A6%86.md">咖啡馆</a>，已成为台中颇具热度的高空景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298267" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/26060295_1013104335497726_6311469925390634519_o-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11298267 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/26060295_1013104335497726_6311469925390634519_o-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1%E4%BB%BB%E5%8A%A1-cafetask.md">咖啡任务 CAFETASK</a> 高空<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1%E9%A6%86.md">咖啡馆</a>室内景观，格调高雅。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/pg/CafeTask/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CafeTask </a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台中市南区的“<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1%E4%BB%BB%E5%8A%A1-cafetask.md">咖啡任务 CAFETASK</a>”，是一家位于36层楼的台中最高<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E8%A7%82%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md">景观餐厅</a>，因其景观优美、价格适宜、美食丰富，被不少网友频频打卡分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/photo-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11298253 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/photo-3-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在位于36楼高层的咖啡任务餐厅，赏<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E7%A9%BA%E6%99%AF%E8%A7%82.md">高空景观</a>，视野绝佳。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/pg/CafeTask/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CafeTask </a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>靠窗位置的景观以其不一样的视野，成为该咖啡厅最抢手的座位，通常需提前预定，否则在就餐时间等候观景座位的人就要排起长队。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/27072279_1032494323558727_5349031236364406117_n-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11298213 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/27072279_1032494323558727_5349031236364406117_n-1-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">绝佳视野赏高空美景，成为该咖啡厅的最大卖点，靠窗的位置需提前预定，否则需排队等候。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/CafeTask/photos/a.961115230696637/1032494323558727/?type=1&amp;theater" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CAFETASK</a> 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>店内提供烘培精致的各色糕点，其中招牌甜点金锅<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md">松饼</a>，因其口味香松醇厚受到很多网友喜爱，其它食物如披萨、可颂等也受顾客青睐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/photo-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11298238 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/photo-1-600x439.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">咖啡任务的餐点美味而价格亲民。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/pg/CafeTask/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CafeTask </a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>店内饮料包括各式香浓咖啡、茶饮料及气泡饮等，咖啡因其口感纯正，较受欢迎。</p>
<p>饮品中柚子茶备受好评，柚子与蜂蜜红茶搭配妙绝，茶汤中加入清新酸爽的滋味，红茶的热度释出果皮清香，饮之口齿留香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/17038582_845647832243378_3992965836702680161_o.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11298334 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/17038582_845647832243378_3992965836702680161_o-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柚子茶口感清新细腻，受到一些顾客的喜爱。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/CafeTask/photos/a.803667473108081/845647832243378/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CafeTask </a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在咖啡任务餐厅享受一份午后甜点与醇香的咖啡，整个台中市景观一览无余，同时任思绪驰骋、创意萦怀；或夜幕降临之际，一些朋友与三两知己好友相约共享浪漫氛围，与商业客户洽谈，都是不错的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/photo-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11298244 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/photo-2-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">凭窗远眺，台中城市风光一览无余。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/pg/CafeTask/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖啡任务 CafeTask</a> 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于这个独特的高空打卡景点，很多网友纷纷留言道：</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“柚子茶香气清新，很细腻的味道，很好喝！”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“气氛很轻松，适合聊天的好地方！”“pizza跟<a href="https://github.com/szzd2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md">松饼</a>好吃！景观窗视野很棒！”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“地方不大，但很甜美；景观位子不多，两个人顾店要多点耐心等候。”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“全台中最高的咖啡店，不收服务费，咖啡好喝还有空画龙猫，松饼好吃到连焦边都不放过！一有靠窗位置马上帮你换，这么优质的店一定要告诉大家啊！”</p>
