<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>震颖堆高机租售保修 30年经营诚实不欺</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/3ed0e5ff4a337de1d585ce9b0f8b8557-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>通过国家认证的外汇堆高机。（震颖企业提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月19日讯】（大纪元记者朴贤子台湾高雄报导）<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">震颖企业</a>行30多年来从经营<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>进出口、出租、买卖、保养、维修，到进口贩售日本优质品牌<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E6%B1%87.md">外汇</a>产品，完善的售前规划与售后服务，再加上<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81.md">租赁</a>重机械项目的多元与保障，深获老客户的信赖，以口碑带动行销，在业界说到<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>就想到“<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">震颖企业行</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11331862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/b82ee63abaa56716d3da3ebbb3578a59.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11331862 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/b82ee63abaa56716d3da3ebbb3578a59-450x465.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="465" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">负责人亲自监督机具下货柜的每一个环节。（震颖企业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了满足现今客户的需求，最高品质且通过国家标准的产品，震颖企业的<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>和重机械取得国家级型式检定合格认证，在使用上不仅安全而且更让人放心。推动以客制化服务为经营主轴，其中日本区域产品为<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>，种类涵盖全新与<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8F%A4.md">中古</a>的直立式电车、柴油<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>、汽油<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>；欧美区域产品为<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E7%8C%AB.md">山猫</a>、高空车。并新增二手铲装机,高空车<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E6%B1%87.md">外汇</a>进口贩售,提供客户一站式购物,满足客户不同需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11331863" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/c523bbecd5ce9c050a324ef2d62868c0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11331863" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/c523bbecd5ce9c050a324ef2d62868c0-600x301.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="301" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">欧美进口<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E7%8C%AB.md">山猫</a>。（震颖企业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">震颖企业</a>专营外汇产品，特别在日本成立了贸易事业“崧咏企业有限公司”，由企业第二代负责，以客制化服务为导向，精心挑选具优良品质的重机械，透过海关交至顾客手中。并为预算有限但对重机械有高品质需求的客户，规划了完善的<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81.md">租赁</a>规划，让客户以较低成本能享受高品质的设备，贴心的服务，给原本担心购买重机械会占据大部分预算的事业主，在开拓生意时有了坚强的后援。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11331865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/0f5e759ab330a405946d5859e879f476.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11331865" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/0f5e759ab330a405946d5859e879f476-600x374.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="374" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">进口直立式<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A0%86%E9%AB%98%E6%9C%BA.md">堆高机</a>。（震颖企业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">震颖企业</a>的理念为“震撼人心，服务新颖”，于民国75年，由现今“高雄市<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A0%86%E9%AB%98%E6%9C%BA.md">堆高机</a>同业安全管理协进会”第一届董事长翁先生以一台小货车起家，，开展<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/paper/other_select_index.php?title_id=2735&amp;group_id=165#left_page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">堆高机</a>买卖、维修服务，一路走来，就是凭着自身专业技术与诚信原则的理念奠定了业界称道的好口碑。</p>
<p>外汇堆高机买卖、欧美高空车与山猫请指名“<a href="http://www.zhenyingcompany.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">震颖企业</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11331867" style="width: 320px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9fe62a9dfc325cb94ae56e85f29c775f.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11331867" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9fe62a9dfc325cb94ae56e85f29c775f-600x257.jpg" alt="" width="320" b="137" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">LOGO图。(震颖企业提供）</figcaption></figure>
