<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《海豚湾恋人》主演戏外重逢 相约沙滩路跑</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729412384-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>徐洁儿（左）、林韦君（右）6月26日在台北出席路跑记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月26日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）偶像剧《<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%B1%9A%E6%B9%BE%E6%81%8B%E4%BA%BA.md">海豚湾恋人</a>》中的沈曼青与徐珊妮戏外重逢，<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%B4%81%E5%84%BF.md">徐洁儿</a>与<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E9%9F%A6%E5%90%9B.md">林韦君</a>今天（26日）一同出席记者会，宣布将参加全台最浪漫的沙滩嘉年华<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%AF%E8%B7%91.md">路跑</a>活动，还要一睹美丽风景、大啖当地美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11347542" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729472384-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729472384-ss1.jpg" alt="徐洁儿" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11347542" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%B4%81%E5%84%BF.md">徐洁儿</a>6月26日在台北出席<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%AF%E8%B7%91.md">路跑</a>记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11347544" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260730062384-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260730062384-ss1.jpg" alt="林韦君" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11347544" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E9%9F%A6%E5%90%9B.md">林韦君</a>6月26日在台北出席路跑记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧当年在澎湖<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E8%B4%9D%E5%B1%BF.md">吉贝屿</a>拍摄，剧中饰演沈曼青与徐珊妮的两位女星合作后成为无话不谈的好闺密，徐洁儿称赞林韦君容貌如昔，而林韦君也称羡她热爱长跑的毅力，对于她健美的身材甘拜下风。</p>
<p>徐洁儿也喊话：“大家不只能参加赛事，而且单身的男女们一定要穿上自己最有自信的战袍，说不定就能在赛事当中找到<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%B1%9A%E6%B9%BE%E6%81%8B%E4%BA%BA.md">海豚湾恋人</a>，一起共度浪漫沙滩嘉年华。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11347545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729302384-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906260729302384-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="徐洁儿（左）、林韦君（右）" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-11347545" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">徐洁儿（左）、林韦君（右）当年因合作《海豚湾恋人》而成为闺密。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
