<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：乘风飞去吧，风筝</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/kite-1209241_1280-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夕阳下的天空，几只风筝在飞；牵着长长细线的，有孩童，也有老人。（公有领域）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>
乘风飞去吧，风筝<br />
飞到更远的地方<br />
飞到更广阔的天地<br />
是欧洲？是美洲？是大洋洲……<br />
是山？是河？是平原……<br />
都可以不管<br />
只要活得快乐<br />
　<br />
挣脱束缚，向无限飞去<br />
有多少风雨都不必计较<br />
即使再苦，再难<br />
即使前程渺茫不可测<br />
都值得去冒险<br />
外面的世界是海阔天空的<br />
那里有许多宝藏任你挖，任你取<br />
　<br />
不要太顾及儿女私情<br />
任何人都需要断奶的<br />
即使成了断线的风筝<br />
又如何？<br />
只要记得有那么一个故乡<br />
别的不去和他太多牵缠<br />
前方的光明最是重要</p>
<p><strong>Soar away, oh Kite</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Soar away, oh Kite<br />
Soar to the place more far<br />
Soar to the field wider<br />
Is it Europe? America? or Oceania?<br />
Is it the mountain? river? or plain?<br />
Only it’s happy<br />
Regardless of where it is</p>
<p>Get rid of the fetter, soar toward limitless<br />
Never haggle of how many wind and rain<br />
Even how bitter and difficult it is<br />
Even it’s uncertain the future<br />
All will worth to take chances<br />
Boundless oceans, vast skies of outside world<br />
You can dig and take what you want</p>
<p>Don’t take in consideration of love affair between man and woman too much<br />
Any body needs wean<br />
Even to be a kite which breaks the string<br />
How will it be?<br />
Only such a birthplace bears in your mind<br />
Don’t to worry about others<br />
The most important thing is the bright of the future@</p>
