<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亮哲体格比起“海蛙”不逊色 莎莎大开眼界</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420522122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019国防节目《能战！全民新视界》8月23日在台北举行。图左起为莎莎、阿基师、亮哲。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月23日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）台湾艺人<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%8E%E8%8E%8E.md">莎莎</a>、<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%AE%E5%93%B2.md">亮哲</a>、<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%9F%BA%E5%B8%88.md">阿基师</a>等今天（23日）出席全新国防节目《能战！全民新视界》记者会，从中华民国的陆军<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%BE%99%E8%9B%99%E5%85%B5.md">海龙蛙兵</a>到空军捍卫战士，再到海军国舰国造，深入训练基地和外岛最前线部队实境拍摄，共推出三大系列、共12集。<br />
<figure id="attachment_11473279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420482122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420482122-ss1-600x351.jpg" alt="能战!全民新视界-海龙蛙兵教官" width="600" b="351" class="size-large wp-image-11473279" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《能战！全民新视界》记者会23日在台北举行。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%AE%E5%93%B2.md">亮哲</a>这次拍摄与<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%BE%99%E5%B0%8F%E7%BB%84.md">神龙小组</a>为伍，尝试了高塔跳伞。10月要去参加铁人三项比赛的他，最近勤练体能，甚至还有八块肌，在现场与<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%BE%99%E8%9B%99%E5%85%B5.md">海龙蛙兵</a>教官PK腹肌及背肌，体格毫不逊色。</p>
<p>一旁的<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%8E%E8%8E%8E.md">莎莎</a>看了直呼：“根本是大开眼界。”她每次进部队录影前一定会先画好妆，笑说：“每天都吃很饱，军中伙食棒之外，眼睛也看饱。”被问及男友会不会吃醋？她笑说：“不会呀，此时不吃待何时！”</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_11473282" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230421092122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230421092122-ss1.jpg" alt="亮哲" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11473282" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">亮哲出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_11473284" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230421032122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230421032122-ss1.jpg" alt="《能战！全民新视界》莎莎" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11473284" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">莎莎出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_11473285" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420402122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420402122-ss1.jpg" alt="阿基师" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11473285" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%9F%BA%E5%B8%88.md">阿基师</a>出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>而阿基师难得走出摄影棚拍摄，看到部队的设备与饮食都嗔舌，伙付加谐师证照，厨技也不输饭店。他自曝以前在部队进伙房，出错闹糗事，原来馒头未做好，数百位弟兄没得吃，结果不仅被馒头丢，最后还被罚在餐厅匍匐前进绕一圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11473288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420432122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1908230420432122-ss1-600x376.jpg" alt="全民新视界" width="600" b="376" class="size-large wp-image-11473288" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《能战！全民新视界》23日在台北举行记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
