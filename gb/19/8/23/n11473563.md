<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>艺协第21届中华舞蹈夏令营展现中国舞之美</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-2ACAS-2-e1566581453128.jpg" />
<h6>学员们展示她们一周以来学习的基本功和舞蹈。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨屋本市报导）8月17日下午，由纽英中华艺术协会(ACAS)主办、中华民国侨委会赞助的“第21届中华<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md">舞蹈</a><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E8%90%A5.md">夏令营</a>”在渥本镇的<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E5%8D%8F.md">艺协</a>中心圆满落幕。19名参与<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E8%90%A5.md">夏令营</a>的孩子为老师、家长们展示她们一周以来学习的基本功和<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md">舞蹈</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11473618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-1ACAS-2-e1566581114818.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11473618" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-1ACAS-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E5%8D%8F.md">艺协</a>“第21届中华舞蹈夏令营”结业式后指导老师、学员、嘉宾合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11473624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-3ACAS-2-e1566581158273.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11473624" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-3ACAS-2-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">学员们展示她们一周以来学习的基本功和舞蹈。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11473627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-4ACAS-2-e1566581303875.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11473627" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-4ACAS-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">学员们展示她们一周以来学习的基本功和舞蹈。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在舞台上，孩子们展示了她们学习的中国舞、芭蕾舞、流行舞等多个舞种技巧，最后还表演了4支中国舞，令台下的家长们纷纷欢呼和录影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11473628" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-5ACAS-2-e1566581343786.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11473628 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-5ACAS-2-450x367.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="367" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长徐佑典致辞。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11473629" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-6ACAS-2-e1566581390867.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11473629 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-2-6ACAS-2-450x417.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="417" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任欧宏伟（左）、艺协艺术总监陈玉律（右）为舞蹈老师Winnie Li (李薇薇)颁奖致谢。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长徐佑典夫妇、侨教中心主任欧宏伟亦到场致词，称赞孩子们的功夫，感谢艺协传承中华舞蹈的努力。</p>
<p>欧宏伟及艺协会长陈玉律还特别向夏令营老师、助教、护士和保管员颁奖致谢。其中多位舞蹈老师、助教也都曾是艺协的学生。◇</p>
