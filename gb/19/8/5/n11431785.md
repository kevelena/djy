<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>又老又病的吉娃娃无依无靠 好心人领回当宝贝</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>霍华德不仅领养了摩托车，还为它开设粉专，希望借此提升大众对于收容所老狗的关注度。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Scooter's Rescue
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年08月05日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君综合报导）一只年迈的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E5%A8%83%E5%A8%83.md">吉娃娃</a>由于遇上主人离世而失去依靠，成了<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B5%AA%E7%8B%97.md">流浪狗</a>，让它差点儿老死<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%AE%B9%E6%89%80.md">收容所</a>。遭逢这么大的变故，它只好每夜钻入棉被睡觉，这成了它最大的安慰。它孤零零的模样令许多人看了鼻酸，却也让新主人决定许它一个永远的家。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800000;">顿失饲主的高龄<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E5%A8%83%E5%A8%83.md">吉娃娃</a>无处去</span></h2>
<p>据动物网媒“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo）报导，12岁的吉娃娃“摩托车”（Scooter）今年7月因主人骤逝，一夜之间无家可归，加上它有髋关节问题，只能住进美国密西根州的动物保护组织“布兰奇县人道之家”（Humane Society of Branch County）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-06.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431840 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-06-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">12岁的吉娃娃“摩托车”，相当于人类的64岁！(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>年纪大又有病痛的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md">小狗</a>，很难找到领养对象，以至于保护组织的员工都想：它可能就要老死在这里了。</p>
<p>即使找到新家庭，这只老吉娃娃能否顺利适应也很难说，更别说与新的饲主相处融洽。</p>
<p>正当大家都一筹莫展时，这家强调“零杀害”的人道主义<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%AE%B9%E6%89%80.md">收容所</a>，将“摩托车”每夜都要“自动”埋进专属被毯的照片放到粉专，许多人都被它孤单的样子触动。</p>
<p><center><a src=></a><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cbzs/1/master/t/ntdtv/facebook.jpg" ><h3 align=center>(facebook视频或图片.国内建议下载<a href="https://git.io/fjmgJ">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/post.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2F103579396402030%2Fphotos%2Fa.671643582928939%2F2384419478317999%2F%3Ftype%3D3&amp;width=700&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=440&amp;appId" width="600" b="377"></a></center></p>
<h2><span style="color: #800000;">她创粉专呼吁关心高龄犬</span></h2>
<p>洁西卡．琳恩．霍华德（Jessica Lynn Howard）便是其中一位忍不住想多关心“摩托车”安危的好心人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431847" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-03.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431847 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-03-450x432.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="432" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">霍华德和丈夫提姆领养了“摩托车”车，并为它开设粉专，希望借此提升大众对于收容所老狗的关注度。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我看着珍（“布兰奇县人道之家”的经理）的脸书贴文，还有世界发生的其它事”，霍华德对动物网媒“渡渡鸟”说，“丈夫提姆（Tim）跟我躺在床上（看），然后这篇贴文??让我哭了。我毫不犹豫地给提姆看文章，接着我说：‘我一定要去带走它’！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-07.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431863 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-07-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“摩托车”与它的吉娃娃同伴：贾克斯（Jax，中）、娃娃脸（Baby Face，右）。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11431870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-08.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431870 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-08-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在霍华德和提姆的照顾下，一把年纪的“摩托车”成功融入新家庭！(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>霍华德过去从“布兰奇县人道之家”领养了两只吉娃娃，家里还是一处农庄。有了同伴，又有完美的环境，“摩托车”可以放心在此“安养天年”了！</p>
<p>“睡觉的时间一到??它还是会把自己塞进（被窝）里面”，霍华德对“渡渡鸟”透露，“我试过帮它放进去，但它更想要自己来。它可以把自己盖得刚刚好。看它这样就觉得很可爱。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431882" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-01.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431882 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-01-450x536.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="536" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“摩托车”不需要别人的帮助，总是能自己将棉被盖好。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11431877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-02.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431877 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-02-450x535.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="535" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一早醒来，棉被里的“摩托车”犹睡眼惺忪。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“摩托车”虽然老了，成天打瞌睡；但是，看到它安稳地待在身边打盹儿，就是霍华德最大的成就！</p>
<p>霍华德为了帮助更多老狗能得到领养机会，便将“摩托车”的点点滴滴记录在脸书粉专“摩托车救援志”（Scooter’s Rescue）上，希望看到的民众也能走进在地收容所，把等着它们的毛孩带回家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431885" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-05.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431885 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190806-Yichun-Scooter-Scooters-Rescue-05-450x799.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">风 霍华德希望透过“摩托车”的平凡故事，让大众愿意走进在地收容所，给老狗一个新家。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scootersrescue/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Scooter&#8217;s Rescue</a>)</figcaption></figure>
