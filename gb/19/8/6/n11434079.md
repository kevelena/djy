<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>耿静：通胀与印钞</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>―- 觉醒！<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A9%E5%91%BD.md">革命</a>！别无选择！</p>
<p>―- Awakening! Revolution! There is no other alternative !</p>
<p>到上世纪中期为止，在大多数国家仍然处在以农业和当地贸易为基础的经济体系时，通货膨胀的确是一个可靠的经济观察。</p>
<p>Until the middle of last century, when most countries were still in an agricultural and local based economy state, inflation was a reliable economic observation.</p>
<p>但是，当现代的制造业效率和贸易进口速度可以随时从外国补给本国市场的农产品、轻工产品甚至部分重工产品的需求时，地区性通货膨胀的压力自然被缓解了。</p>
<p>However, when modern manufacturing efficiency and the speedy import can replenish the local market with immediate supply of agricultural goods, light industrial goods and even some heavy industrial products from foreign land, the pressure of local inflation diminishes.</p>
<p>在此恳请大家的警惕，当某一个集权政府可以通过<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E9%92%9E.md">印钞</a>、进口、转基因、人造食品等方法及时补给本国市场需求时，通货膨胀很难显像。</p>
<p>I would like to call for people’s attention! When a tyranny can endlessly satisfy local market demand using the means of speedy import, GMO, artificial food in addition to currency manipulation, inflation will not be apparent.</p>
<p>所以！千万不要低估共产党<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E9%92%9E.md">印钞</a>加上汇率操纵的勾当对国际秩序的扰乱！</p>
<p>Therefore! We should not judge lightly for Chinese Government’s currency over-printing tactic plus currency manipulation, and must realize it’s horrific capacity of disturbance to international order.</p>
<p>换言之，疾病污染，资源枯竭，文化凋亡，只是最轻的后果！</p>
<p>Illness and pollution, resource depletion, culture apoptosis, these are only the lightest consequences.</p>
<p>通货膨胀的经济衰败促使集权体制崩塌的希望，是错误的！是无望的！</p>
<p>It is hopeless and wrongful relying your hope only on the possibility of self-destruction of a tyranny by economic hard landing!</p>
<p>觉醒！<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A9%E5%91%BD.md">革命</a>！别无选择！</p>
<p>Awakening! Revolution! There is no other alternative !</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%BF%E9%9D%99.md">耿静</a></p>
<p>Jing Geng</p>
