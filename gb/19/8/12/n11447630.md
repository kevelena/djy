<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东湾川味馆攻略</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.07.46-600x400.jpg" />

</h6>

<p>【大纪元2019年08月12日讯】文：勤宜?大纪元</p>
<h2><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E5%B7%9D%E5%91%B3.md">东湾川味</a>：川福楼<br />
Sichuan Fortune House</h2>
<p>“我和太太每周都来这家餐馆，我们在亚洲很多国家生活、旅行过，这家餐馆的味道确实很不错。”食客推荐菜品有辣子鸡、糖醋鱼、香酥牛肉、腊肉炒蒜苗、回锅肉、宫保鸡丁。这是东湾一家比较传统的<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9D%E8%8F%9C%E9%A6%86.md">川菜馆</a>，包括<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9B%E5%B7%9D%E5%B0%8F%E5%90%83.md">四川小吃</a>和一些混搭的菜品。服务周到，也是一个不错的家庭聚餐选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11447770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20180405132518.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11447770" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20180405132518-600x288.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="288" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炒饭虽然不是川菜，但也是川福楼热门餐点。（<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E5%B7%9D%E5%91%B3.md">东湾川味</a>，川福楼网站）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11447773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1.22.33.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11447773" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1.22.33-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东湾川味，川福楼雅致的座椅。（谷歌地图截图）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>东湾<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9D%E8%8F%9C%E9%A6%86.md">川菜馆</a>：辣妹子Spices 3</h2>
<p>位于奥克兰中国城的辣妹子3，胜在价格经济，服务快捷周到，而且有重庆、成都、上海、台湾等口味。有食客推荐，比如麻辣酥鱼片、干煸四季豆、红油抄手等。停车位也好找。如果吃一顿经济实惠的川味，这家可以考虑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11447775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20130814063230.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11447775" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20130814063230-450x434.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="434" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">辣妹子3有各地的辣味。（东湾川菜馆，辣妹子3网站）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11447777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20130814063202.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11447777" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20130814063202-450x472.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="472" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四川凉粉。（东湾川菜馆，辣妹子3网站）</figcaption></figure>
<p>辣妹子联络方式：<br />
地址：370 12th Street<br />
Oakland, CA 94607<br />
电话：510-625-8889<br />
网址：<a href="http://spices3oakland.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">spices3oakland.com</a></p>
<h2>东湾川菜馆，中国餐厅<br />
China House Restaurant</h2>
<p>感受中国古色古香的精美装饰，是这家餐馆的一个亮点，也是吸引客户的亮点。中午套餐很受欢迎。从头台到最后的甜品冰淇淋，让人感觉很完美。一位老客户更是把这里作为和父亲聚会的固定餐馆，“坐在临窗的位置，和父亲一起品尝美食，绝对是一种享受。”这家更像是川菜和粤菜的混搭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11447779" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1.57.37.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11447779" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1.57.37-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中国餐厅的窗边座位很热门。（东湾川菜馆，中国餐厅谷歌地图截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11447780" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1.58.03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11447780" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1.58.03-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">提供个人餐点，有别于一般中餐馆的大菜盘。（东湾川菜馆，中国餐厅谷歌地图截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国餐厅联络方式：<br />
地址：2328 Santa Clara Ave.<br />
Alameda, CA 94501<br />
电话：510-521-2500</p>
<h2>东湾川味：一品川湘<br />
Sichuan Style Restaurant</h2>
<p>喜欢并了解川菜的话，那就多花一点点钱到这家尝试一下。有对川菜有经验的食客点评：这家的菜非常地道，麻辣味的程度非常到位。除了非常有川菜特色的藤椒鱼、水煮系列，还有京味十足的烤鸭和一些粤式小吃。一位经营巴西咖啡馆的店主说：“这是他吃过最好吃的川菜馆。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11447781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.07.46.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11447781" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.07.46-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一品川湘的五彩大拉皮。（东湾川味，一品川湘谷歌地图截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11447782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.18.42.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11447782" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.18.42-450x664.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="664" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四川香肠。（东湾川味，一品川湘谷歌地图截图）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>东湾川味：古月飘香Chengdu Style Restaurant</h2>
<p>可能是一家更有成都特色的家常川菜小吃馆，各色串串、凉粉、凉面、红油抄手等特色川味小吃。除了小吃，也有香辣蟹、辣子鸡、麻辣牙签肉和水煮系列等川菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11447783" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.29.19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11447783" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2.29.19-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">古月飘香的麻辣串串。（东湾川味，古月飘香谷歌地图截图/vivian zhu）</figcaption></figure>
