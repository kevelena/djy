<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠中秋庆典喜迎50周年</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-1Mid-Autumn-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>由纽英中华公所主办的第50届“华埠中秋节联欢会”，吸引成千上万游客。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md">波士顿</a>报导）由纽英中华公所主办的波士顿“华埠<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B.md">中秋</a>节<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E6%AC%A2.md">联欢</a>会”，今年已是50周年了。8月11日的第50届庆典中，诸多表演、工艺、文化和商贩摊位吸引成千上万游客挤满华埠街道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11458630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-2Mid-Autumn-2-e1565982931388.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458630" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-2Mid-Autumn-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">舞台的舞狮表演吸引了众多观众围观。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11458641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-6Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983250304.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458641" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-6Mid-Autumn-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">彩龙游街。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上午10点，华埠Harrison大道菲利浦广场的舞台上响起锣鼓，洪青体育会舞狮拉开庆典序幕。彩师丢出象征发财成功的橙子和白菜，吸引观众们尖叫、抢接。中华公所职员雷国辉、周树昂为司仪；邝坤珍、朱苏珊、司徒文信、陈文浩等成员也在鼎力帮忙。</p>
<p>在接下来6个小时的表演中，茉莉花、康乃馨等歌舞团体，超武馆、黄氏、华林、阴阳道等武馆和醒狮队纷纷带来舞狮和武术表演，吸引密密麻麻的群众在台前观赏。各武馆的醒狮队在登台前，还先在华埠街道举旗巡游，吸引人们夹道围观、拍照。</p>
<p>在“天下为公”牌楼外的小舞台上，君子楼粤曲社、广东音乐社、侨声音乐社和仙艺曲乐社也依次带来传统曲目演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11458638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-5Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983162316.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458638" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-5Mid-Autumn-2-600x375.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾与中华公所职员合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>到场祝贺的嘉宾包括麻州众议员陈德基、<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md">波士顿</a>市议员费连、吴弭、消防局副局长黄瑞瑜、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长徐佑典、侨教中心主任欧宏伟，以及赞助商代表等等。费连和吴弭还带来市议会赠予主办方中华公所的贺状。</p>
<p>“祝福大家在这个收获之月里享有欢愉，以及度过愉快的一天。事实上，今天的天气非常非常不错！”陈德基说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11458654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-10Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983505700.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458654" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-10Mid-Autumn-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲会展出客家文化。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天，Harrison、Beach、Hudson、Tyler等华埠主要街道都设有文化和商贩摊位，总计约60个摊位参与庆典，多于去年。本报亦在Beach街向各族裔游客介绍英文大纪元(The Epoch Times)媒体。</p>
<p>阮鸿灿、李奇舜、李耀培、关美玲、刘华权等等治安巡逻队义工穿插人群之中，确保现场安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11458632" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-3Mid-Autumn-e1565983024255.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11458632 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-3Mid-Autumn-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">璞石中华文化工作室义工教孩子们制作捏面小猪。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11458636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-4Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983090796.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458636" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-4Mid-Autumn-2-600x352.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾与汉字文化节书法家合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11458652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-9Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983441197.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458652" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-9Mid-Autumn-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">彩画形式书写出艺术名字很受欢迎。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>璞石中华文化工作室现场写书法、教孩子们制作捏面小猪，吸引不少人围观和动手。还有中国结手环也很抢手，人们对中国传统手工艺很喜欢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11458648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-8Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983349104.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458648" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-8Mid-Autumn-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">华埠狮子会的免费验光车。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如往年一般，华埠狮子会会长谢如键及多位成员把移动验光车(Eye Mobile)停在Beach街路边，为人们免费检查眼睛，吸引许多人排队等候“上车”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11458644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-7Mid-Autumn-2-e1565983303516.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11458644" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/1-1-7Mid-Autumn-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龙舟模拟器，挑战者奋力划桨，吸引人们围观和喝采。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>附近的Wolfpack龙舟队带来龙舟模拟器，邀路人在模拟200米的河道上决出最快桨手；参与者铆足力气划桨，围观人群也激动地喝采。</p>
<p>相较于去年该活动碰到一早的倾盆大雨，迫使有些摊位空置；前年的近百度高温，让有些人裹足不前，今年的天气是最佳的清朗不冷不热，所以人潮不断。◇</p>
