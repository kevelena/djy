<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鸡笼中元祭游行  法轮大法队伍受欢迎</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/c584bb356ee66a6608522b6140c9605d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>法轮大法大型花车。（林志彦／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月15日讯】（大纪元记者陈秀媛台湾基隆报导）传承165年的鸡笼<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%83%E7%A5%AD.md">中元祭</a>，14日晚间在基隆市区进举行放水灯游行，总统蔡英文与基隆市长林右昌共同揭开序幕，各姓氏宗亲会的主题<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%BD%A6.md">花车</a>、灯车及演出团队盛大登场，其中以<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%A4%A7%E6%B3%95.md">法轮大法</a>的大型花车、<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%9B%BD%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%A2.md">天国乐团</a>、<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E9%BC%93%E9%98%9F.md">腰鼓队</a>及仙女队组成的庞大队伍，最受瞩目，沿途民众夹道欢迎、热闹滚滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11453953" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/a56514f880f8f6c93491acd6f936f047.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11453953" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/a56514f880f8f6c93491acd6f936f047-450x352.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="352" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%83%E7%A5%AD.md">中元祭</a>放水灯游行开幕的精彩演出。（林志彦／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中元祭放水灯游行开幕，特别邀请乱弹阿翔、林雅雪、采风乐坊及大红桔剧团协力演出；整场演出以“乐声呼唤?时代拼搏”为题，融合音乐、舞蹈、鼓声、武术等表演元素，以最精彩的演出，展现中元祭165年积累的文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11453956" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/bbb166d8a637018c48ac9a63413b0c0e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11453956" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/bbb166d8a637018c48ac9a63413b0c0e-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">许姓宗亲会的大型<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%BD%A6.md">花车</a>。 （林志彦／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡英文表示，鸡笼中元祭能代代相传，靠的是各宗亲会的无私奉献，为了让鸡笼中元祭更加发扬保存，中央去年特别补助经费，协助主普坛整建，让基隆有现代的建设，也有传统的文化。</p>
<p>林右昌强调，鸡笼中元祭是台湾的文化资产，过去先民在这片土地上打拼，后来以拼阵头代替打破头，直到今日由15个宗亲会轮流主办中元祭，展现先民化解纷争，转化为社会和谐力量的智慧，希望也能转化为台湾向前迈进的力量，促进台湾和谐、全民团结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11453955" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/71d61277580a5e04f361b02531fbc792.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11453955" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/71d61277580a5e04f361b02531fbc792-450x407.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="407" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蔡英文、林右昌与游行队伍一起，宣告活动正式开始。（林志彦／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>精彩的开场演出后，蔡英文与林右昌一起致赠“阐弘文化”匾额给今年轮值主普的许姓宗亲会，随后在基隆市警察局重型机车队的前导下，蔡英文、林右昌与许姓宗亲会率领的游行队伍一起，宣告游行活动正式开始。各姓宗亲会及社团精心制作的花车与精彩的表演队伍绕行基隆市区，吸引民众争相观赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11453959" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/d31a4e5d9f98d7fa8cd6ca13a86198f9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11453959" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/d31a4e5d9f98d7fa8cd6ca13a86198f9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%9B%BD%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%A2.md">天国乐团</a>的精彩演奏。（林志彦／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11453960" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/b8cc5040578003fae4684171968cd0a2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11453960" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/b8cc5040578003fae4684171968cd0a2-450x264.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="264" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E9%BC%93%E9%98%9F.md">腰鼓队</a>的精彩演出。（林志彦／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11453962" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/a493de52094e33ad7d3868f7338e60a5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11453962" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/a493de52094e33ad7d3868f7338e60a5-450x272.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="272" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">仙女队沿途发放的精美莲花。（林志彦／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绵延几公里的游行队伍中，以台湾<a href="https://github.com/dfcbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%A4%A7%E6%B3%95.md">法轮大法</a>学会的大型花车、天国乐团、腰鼓队及仙女队组成的庞大队伍，最受瞩目，尤其天国乐团及腰鼓队的高水准演出，一再得到民众的鼓掌与欢呼，仙女队沿途发放的精美莲花，更是人见人爱。◇</p>
