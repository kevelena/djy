<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>故事行销点子棒 收容所帮“星际宝贝”找到家</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-14-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”将里面的猫猫狗狗打扮成星际宝贝的模样，成功激发民众的领养意愿。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">OKC Animal Welfare
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年08月05日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君综合报导）美国奥克拉荷马州的动物<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%AE%B9%E6%89%80.md">收容所</a>为了鼓励民众前往认养，将里头的猫猫狗狗打扮成“星际宝贝”，呼吁大众不要只关心<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/51%E5%8C%BA.md">51区</a>的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E6%98%9F%E4%BA%BA.md">外星人</a>，地球还有很多可爱的动物等着你我关心！</p>
<p>美国网民近期在脸书上号召众人一窥军事秘密基地“<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/51%E5%8C%BA.md">51区</a>”（Area 51），引发百万人蜂拥而至，也触动了美国政府的敏感神经，提醒民众如果真闯禁区，很可能吃上官司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11430997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-01.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11430997 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-01-600x600.jpg" alt="“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”从“风暴51区”的荒谬发想中，找到了吸引潜在领养者的妙计，帮助许多收容所里的小狗找到好人家。(Courtesy of OKC Animal Welfare)" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”从“风暴51区”的荒谬发想中，找到了吸引潜在领养者的妙计，帮助许多<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E5%AE%B9%E6%89%80.md">收容所</a>里的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md">小狗</a>找到好人家。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>但是，这个看似荒谬的活动，却激发“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”（OKC Animal Welfare）找到了吸引潜在领养者的妙计。</p>
<p>“勇闯我们的收容所吧??我们有了不起的地球毛孩，准备好要保护你免受51区<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E6%98%9F%E4%BA%BA.md">外星人</a>的伤害。 领养它们没有你想像的那么遥不可及！”动物之家的代表在脸书帖文写道。</p>
<p>“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”竟然想出将猫猫狗狗打扮成“星际宝贝”的方法！它们戴上锡箔纸头盔、置身于宇宙的照片一经脸书释出，便获得了网民热烈回响，已近九千人按赞！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-02-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431006 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-02-1-600x618.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="618" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">因为“勇闯收容所”活动而幸运得到领养的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md">小狗</a>。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11431015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-04.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431015 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-04-600x740.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="740" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">因为“勇闯收容所”活动而幸运得到领养的小猫。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>没多久，“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”发文说明帖子里的小狗已经幸运找到好人家了！这次活动也为动物之家募集了超过2900美元（逾9万新台币）。</p>
<p>为了增加其他待领养动物的曝光率，动物之家除了感谢网民的分享和善款，也希望分享粉专照片的人，能够顺手标记“#stormtheshelterOKC”。</p>
<p>“让我们继续让大众明白，领养并不是那么遥远的事，请支持在地收容所！”动物之家在脸书帖文继续鼓励民众领养。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431023" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-05.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431023 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-05-600x776.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="776" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”帮助提升动物领养率的方法既有创意又有效率！(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11431026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431026 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-11-600x729.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="729" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”尽情打扮收容所里的小狗！(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”不断发文分享领养的成功案例，许多小狗小猫都陆续觅得永远的家。这里的毛孩在送到新家前，会先施行绝育手术、接种疫苗和治疗寄生虫，并且会植入晶片，防止它们丢失。</p>
<p>“上周六的活动非常顺利，141只动物找到了他们的新家。”动物之家对《大纪元》说。这个十分有创意的收容所正在积极筹划一系列活动，帮弃猫浪犬与善心人牵起一段美好的缘分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431029 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-adopted-dogs-cats-OKC-Animal-Welfare-13-600x804.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="804" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">越来越多猫狗因为“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”的创意与热情，顺利找到领养家庭。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“OKC做得好啊，这太棒了！ 我期待很多人前往领养”、“为你们团队帮这些毛孩找到好家庭，所想出的妙计道声恭喜。这可能是我见过的最好的行销点子！ 干得好”，许多网民流下了许多鼓励的话，有的还附上了领养的宠物照片，支持动物之家的妙计！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11431034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-to-be-adopted-Daisy-313178-3-OKC-Animal-Welfare-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11431034 size-large" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190802-Yichun-to-be-adopted-Daisy-313178-3-OKC-Animal-Welfare-10-600x742.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="742" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”，仍有许多猫狗亟欲找到领养家庭。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OKCanimalwelfare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKC Animal Welfare</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前，“奥克拉荷马市动物之家”开放民众前往领养的时间是每天中午至下午5:30，猫狗领养费用一般为60美元。假如是四岁以上的猫狗，且待领养时间超过两周，领养之后需要一定程度的医疗照顾，动物之家将视情况减少费用。</p>
