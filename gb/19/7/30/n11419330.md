<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄臣纬Ｓ刖缱樾δ 导演也自认失控</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431572122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《遗失的1/2》首播记者会7月30日在台北举行。图左起为陈玺安、黄臣巍⑼跫伊骸＃黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月30日讯】（大纪元记者黄钟台北报导）《16个夏天》金钟导演<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B8%E5%AF%8C%E7%BF%94.md">许富翔</a>与金钟视后<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%A7%B5%E5%98%89.md">黄臣</a>合作，30日出席新戏《<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%97%E5%A4%B1%E7%9A%8412.md">遗失的1/2</a>》记者会，31岁的黄臣卧以客台文学剧《台北歌手》敲响金钟，这次她为戏特别学习跆拳道，从高中一路演到出社会的不同阶段，她笑称：“大家都蛮疯的，经常互相恶搞，拍戏时都会自行加戏。”<br />
<figure id="attachment_11419389" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431232122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431232122-ss1.jpg" alt="“遗失的1/2”首播记者会。图为金钟视后黄臣巍" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11419389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金钟视后<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%A7%B5%E5%98%89.md">黄臣</a>。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<figure id="attachment_11419392" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431172122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431172122-ss1.jpg" alt="金钟奖导演许富翔“遗失的1/2”首播记者会" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11419392" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金钟奖导演<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B8%E5%AF%8C%E7%BF%94.md">许富翔</a>。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演许富翔自认是片场中最失控的人，笑说：“会笑场在所难免。”爱妻陈意涵也经常到剧组探班、请大家吃东西。</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E7%8E%BA%E5%AE%89.md">陈玺安</a>首度担纲男主角，今年还入选2019十大杰出在美华人青年。他深情守护的角色，透露有场哭戏时间长，他为了入戏，特地携带一罐威士忌到现场饮用，没想到从开拍到结束他竟然把一整罐烈酒给干了，让众人傻眼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11419402" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431332122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431332122-ss1.jpg" alt="陈玺安“遗失的1/2”首播记者会" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11419402" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E7%8E%BA%E5%AE%89.md">陈玺安</a>出席新戏《<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%97%E5%A4%B1%E7%9A%8412.md">遗失的1/2</a>》首播记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11419399" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300432012122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300432012122-ss1.jpg" alt="陈玺安、黄臣巍耙攀У1/2”首播记者会" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11419399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（左起）陈玺安、黄臣纬鱿新戏《遗失的1/2》首播记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11419400" style="width: 392px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300432042122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300432042122-ss1.jpg" alt="林哲熹、韩笙笙“遗失的1/2”首播记者会" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-11419400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（左起）林哲熹、韩笙笙出席新戏《遗失的1/2》首播记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧卡司还有新生代演员林哲熹、韩笙笙、王家梁等人，谱出夏季恋曲。</p>
<p>剧情描述男女主角在冥冥之中有着切不断的缘分，寻找生命的另外一半。女主角发生意外后，藉由一连串事件来找回学生时代的好友们， 并惭悔告白来拉近已经远离的每一段友情，最终迈向新人生的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11419403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431542122-ss1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907300431542122-ss1-600x432.jpg" alt="“遗失的1/2”首播记者会" width="600" b="432" class="size-large wp-image-11419403" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新戏《遗失的1/2》首播记者会7月30日在台北举行。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
