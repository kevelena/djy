<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>奶猫获救立即“抱紧”警官 让他有了新主意</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-04-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>救出小猫后，它们显然也爱上了好心的员警，一只紧紧靠在阿德金斯的颈窝，一只乖乖待在他温暖的臂弯里。（courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ArlingtonPolice/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Arlington Police Department
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年07月12日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君综合报导）美国德州有两名<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F.md">警察</a>在执勤后，接到紧急电话，希望他们能协助救援两只困在车底下的小奶猫。任务完成后，小奶猫紧紧依偎在警察大叔怀中，软萌的模样让警察决定将它们永远收编！</p>
<p>2019年5月，阿灵顿（Arlington）市警局在脸书专页上新增一则“甜蜜蜜”的帖文，其中透露，敏顿（Jesse Minton）和阿德金斯（Joe Bob Adkins）两位警官刚处理完发生在“TJ Maxx”百货的一起偷窃案，旋即收到求救信息――百货公司停车场内，有两只小奶猫受困在车底下。</p>
<p>两名警官原本就要开车离开，没想到一名女士走到他们面前，希望员警能帮忙救出困在车底下的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8C%AB.md">小猫</a>。当他们找到小猫受困的车辆，便听到车底下传来一阵微弱的“喵呜”声；一蹲下，便看到小猫也对着他们瞧。</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8C%AB.md">小猫</a>看到<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F.md">警察</a>大叔，更用力喵喵叫，似乎知道两位警察是好人、准备要救它们出去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11380997" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-02.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11380997 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-02-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿德金斯警官从车底的保险杆救出小猫，他们将成为彼此生命的一部分。(Courtesy of Arlington Police Department)</figcaption></figure>
<p>据动物网媒“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo）报导，“他们在保险杆处发现小猫，很有可能是母猫因为某种原因将小猫们放置在里头”，敏顿表示，“汽车的主人说自己并没有养猫，不清楚小猫为何会跑到那里面。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11381048" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11381048" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小猫显然爱上了好心的员警，一只紧紧靠在阿德金斯的颈窝，一只乖乖待在他温暖的臂弯里。（Courtesy of Arlington Police Department）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿德金斯将两只跟棉花团一样的小奶猫救出后，便带到兽医院检查，结果显示两只小猫约六周大，健康情况良好。他拿起奶瓶喂给小猫配方奶，看着看着，警察大叔心中有了打算。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11381068" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-05.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11381068 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-05-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">经过兽医诊断，两只犹如棉花团的小奶猫约六周大，健康状况良好，模样相当可爱。（Courtesy of Arlington Police Department）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿德金斯决定领养两只奶猫，成为它们的新主人，给小猫一个能够遮风避雨、不愁吃穿的家。他还想以商场“TJ Maxx”为小猫起名“TJ”与“Max”，纪念那一天平凡却无比幸福的出勤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11380994" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-01.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11380994 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190712-Yichun-Kittens-ArlingtonPoliceDepartment-01-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">警察敏顿与阿德金斯（右）成功将小猫救出。(Courtesy of Arlington Police Department)</figcaption></figure>
<p>责任编辑：苏明真</p>
