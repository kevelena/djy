<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Carpark-sale-Enter-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部Carpark sale大促销。（杨裔飞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月08日讯】从6月21日起至6月30日，<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md">澳洲</a><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B7%9E.md">昆州</a>Harvey Norman Oxley的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%99%A8%E9%83%A8.md">电器部</a>，在商场的停车场举办了一场为期十天的半年度<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E4%BF%83%E9%94%80.md">大促销</a>。大促销的产品有各种品牌的厨房小电器、灶具、烤炉、洗衣机和冰箱等，一些商品按样品和工厂厂检瑕疵品的价格销售，折扣最高可达五折。</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%99%A8%E9%83%A8.md">电器部</a>老板Rob Purcell在Harvey Norman有二十多年的工作经验，担任Oxley电器部业主已八年多，对客户的需求十分了解。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11370846" style="width: 502px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Oxley-Carpark-Sale.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11370846" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Oxley-Carpark-Sale.jpg" alt="澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销" width="502" b="228" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md">澳洲</a><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B7%9E.md">昆州</a>Harvey Norman Oxley电器部Carpark sale<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E4%BF%83%E9%94%80.md">大促销</a>。（杨裔飞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11370847" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Rob-Purcell.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11370847" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Rob-Purcell.jpg" alt="澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销" width="501" b="224" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Harvey Norman Oxley电器部老板Rob Purcell（右）和阿里斯顿昆士兰州经销经理Shaun Ruhle。（杨裔飞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rob的经营理念是：“最好的价格、最好的服务”，为顾客提供高水准的专业服务。比如店内有咖啡机操作和制作咖啡的现场展示、烹饪现场展示，让顾客在选购商品的同时，帮助顾客了解产品的性能和使用方法，还能免费享受到美味的咖啡和美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11370842" style="width: 349px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Ariston.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11370842" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Ariston.jpg" alt="澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销" width="349" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Harvey Norman Oxley大促销中带有五年保修的意大利阿里斯顿烤炉。（杨裔飞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11370848" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Sharp-microwave.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11370848" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Sharp-microwave.jpg" alt="澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销" width="350" b="781" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Harvey Norman Oxley大促销中的夏普微波炉。（杨裔飞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rob正与Shaun――意大利阿里斯顿品牌（Ariston）电器昆士兰州经销经理合作，向广大顾客以非常优惠的价格推广阿里斯顿品牌烤箱，而且产品享有五年保修。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11370849" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Stainless-steel-pot.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11370849" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Stainless-steel-pot.jpg" alt="澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销" width="350" b="598" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Harvey Norman Oxley大促销中的五件套不P钢锅具。（杨裔飞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Harvey Norman Oxley的展示厅是澳洲昆州Harvey Norman所有商场中最大的展示厅，一般每年有三次carpark sale活动：半年度、11月的黑色星期五、店庆，为期一般为四天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11370851" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Sunbeam.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11370851" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190708-Chi-Jin-Sunbeam.jpg" alt="澳洲昆州Harvey Norman Oxley电器部大促销" width="351" b="839" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Harvey Norman Oxley大促销中的Sunbeam四片烤面包机。（杨裔飞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
