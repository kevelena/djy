<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东湾日式拉面馆攻略</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/ffee2447b152494b43d9816faaea83c8_XL-10-600x400.jpg" />

</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月22日讯】文：勤宜?大纪元</p>
<h2><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md">东湾日式拉面</a>：Shinmai</h2>
<p>泡在热乎乎的骨头汤的拉面，不喜欢吃拉面的人来这里吃过几次后就成了常客了，也爱上拉面了。也有客人赞叹说这里的拉面，简直是好吃到恨不得给它6颗星！人气旺，服务好，从开胃小吃到拉面的品质和味道，都令食客满意。餐馆生意不错，如果不想等位的话就提前预定吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11401544" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2ddff3dee936e6409bbf3577aa54a740.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11401544" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2ddff3dee936e6409bbf3577aa54a740-450x431.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="431" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Shinmai的拉面。（<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md">东湾日式拉面</a>Shinmai网站截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11401545" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/22330e99e1eec469c910d405a1c1edcd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11401545" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/22330e99e1eec469c910d405a1c1edcd-450x468.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="468" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Shinmai的用餐优雅高尚。（东湾日式拉面Shinmai网站截图）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md">旧金山东湾拉面</a>：Kiraku</h2>
<p>餐馆不大，人气超旺，食客之间的距离让你想起那些热闹的日本小店――食客们并肩享受拉面，气氛很好。这么旺的人气，味道如何就不难断定了。牛舌、烤鸭、铁板鱿鱼等都不错，也算弥补了没有招牌菜的缺憾。而且服务员敏捷而专业，看起来很多等位的客人，但并不会等很长时间。据说这里是伯克利最具有居酒屋（Izakaya）风格的拉面馆。小吃、饮料、拉面还有甜点，让客人食欲大开，吃完了都会再来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11401547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/50519866_2124445854314291_3347962814374346752_n-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11401547" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/50519866_2124445854314291_3347962814374346752_n-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在Kiraku体验日本的居酒屋文化。（旧金山东湾拉面Kiraku脸书）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11401548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/525936_310485492377012_588486387_n-8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11401548" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/525936_310485492377012_588486387_n-8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乌龙炒面。（旧金山东湾拉面Kiraku脸书）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E9%A4%90.md">东湾日本餐</a>：Marufuku Ramen</h2>
<p>好味而独具特色的拉面馆。如果担心猪肉的脂肪，那你可以选择鸡肉拉面，搭配鸡汤，非常美味。食客感言：“怎么早没发现这家拉面馆啊！”大有相见恨晚的遗憾。有食客觉得这家是湾区最棒的拉面馆，在旧金山也有一家。这家也是一家店面小巧的拉面馆，一吃就感觉很惊喜！而且价格也好。喜欢浓味的，也有辣味伺候。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11401549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Marufuku-104_2final.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11401549" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Marufuku-104_2final-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Marufuku提供正宗博多猪骨拉面。（<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E9%A4%90.md">东湾日本餐</a>Marufuku Ramen网站）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>东湾日式拉面：Ramen Hiroshi</h2>
<p>浓稠的奶白汤，有些食客感觉很享受，但也有另外一些食客觉得味道太重，包括盐味。一位早年在夏威夷吃过拉面的客人认为，这家店的拉面才是真真切切的拉面，因为让他回忆起久违了的夏威夷拉面馆的滋味。所谓众口难调，口味重一些的食客可能会喜欢。特别值得一提的是，这家餐馆都采用放养的有机鸡肉，其它食材也同样是采用高品质食材来做各式料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11401559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/ffee2447b152494b43d9816faaea83c8_XL-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11401559 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/ffee2447b152494b43d9816faaea83c8_XL-10-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">招牌豚骨拉面。（东湾日式拉面Ramen Hiroshi网站）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>东湾日式拉面：Yuzu Ramen &amp; Broffee</h2>
<p>这家提供自制面条，面粉中添加日本特有发酵乳酸，使面条更有嚼劲。骨汤是精心熬制30小时而成，强调不加味精或添加剂。满意的食客甚至不希望有太多人知道这家拉面馆，免得自己再去时要排很长的队伍。不过，这家店的价位偏高一些，所以，可能也避免了太过拥挤。当地吃过的食客都公认是本地区最好的拉面馆。</p>
