<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：华府水上花园 夏日荷花盛开</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182200100699-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>肯尼渥兹水上花园（Kenilworth Aquatic Gardens）荷花盛开。（林乐予/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月12日讯】（大纪元记者林乐予美国<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E7%9B%9B%E9%A1%BFdc.md">华盛顿DC</a>报导）华盛顿DC的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%AF%E5%B0%BC%E6%B8%A5%E5%85%B9%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E8%8A%B1%E5%9B%AD.md">肯尼渥兹水上花园</a>（Kenilworth Aquatic Gardens）是全美唯一一家种植<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E8%8A%B1.md">荷花</a>和<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9D%A1%E8%8E%B2.md">睡莲</a>等水生植物的公园，每年7月这里都会举办荷花节，吸引了大批游客前来赏花。</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%AF%E5%B0%BC%E6%B8%A5%E5%85%B9%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E8%8A%B1%E5%9B%AD.md">肯尼渥兹水上花园</a>占地十数英亩，种植了粉色、白色和黄色的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E8%8A%B1.md">荷花</a>及<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9D%A1%E8%8E%B2.md">睡莲</a>，小路旁散漫着灿烂的野花。蝴蝶、蜻蜓和蜜蜂在花间穿梭，蛙鸣声不绝于耳。长过一人高的莲叶接天蔽日，放眼望去，似乎看不到尽头，好一番“接天莲叶无穷碧，映日荷花别样红”的景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11379437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182255100699.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11379437 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182255100699-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肯尼渥兹水上花园（Kenilworth Aquatic Gardens）荷花盛开。（林乐予/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11379438" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182234100699.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11379438 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182234100699-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肯尼渥兹水上花园（Kenilworth Aquatic Gardens）荷花盛开。（林乐予/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6月至8月是观赏荷花的最佳季节，太阳升起后的三小时内是荷花形态最美的时间。一朵荷花的花期通常只有三天，早上开放，傍晚闭合，两三天后就会逐渐凋零。在池塘里很难见到像樱花开放那样“全盛”的景象，因为荷花是一朵一朵地陆续开放，可以慢慢品味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11379443" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182303100699.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11379443 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182303100699-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一朵荷花的花期通常只有三天，早上开放，傍晚闭合。（林乐予/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11379444" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182149100699.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11379444 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182149100699-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肯尼渥兹水上花园（Kenilworth Aquatic Gardens）荷花盛开。（林乐予/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11379447" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182226100699.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11379447 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182226100699-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肯尼渥兹水上花园（Kenilworth Aquatic Gardens）荷花盛开。（林乐予/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本周末（7月13日至14日），肯尼渥兹水上花园将举办“荷花睡莲节”（Lotus and Water Lily Festival），除了赏花，还有多样的文化表演和娱乐节目，是夏日消暑的好去处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11379460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182141100699.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11379460 size-medium" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190711182141100699-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">游客用手机拍下美丽的荷花。（林乐予/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
