<h6>欧洲最大的水上娱乐中心热带岛（Tropical Islands）。（Tropical Islands）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月08日讯】（大纪元记者<span class="TextRun SCXW11146167 BCX4" lang="ZH-CN" xml:lang="ZH-CN" data-contrast="none"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW11146167 BCX4">王亦笑德国报导）</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW11146167 BCX4" data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span><span data-contrast="none">炎炎夏日，正是痛快戏水、享受清凉的最佳时机。这就为您盘点德国评选的最值得去玩的<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%AD.md">水上乐园</a>，找个周末全家人一起去来个乐趣无限的水上之旅，感受无与伦比的舒爽。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><strong>1. <a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E5%B8%A6%E5%B2%9B%E4%B9%90%E5%9B%AD.md">热带岛乐园</a>（Tropical Islands）</strong></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">距离柏林</span><span data-contrast="none">60</span><span data-contrast="none">公里的</span><span data-contrast="none">Krausnick</span><span data-contrast="none">城镇，有欧洲最大的水上娱乐中心，叫做热带岛（</span><span data-contrast="none">Tropical Islands</span><span data-contrast="none">）。这里充满异域风情，白沙滩、棕榈树、随处可见的瀑布，都让人感到已经置身于东南亚小岛的度假胜地。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">最大的亮点当属德国最长的</span><span data-contrast="none">漂流水道</span><span data-contrast="none">，长达</span><span data-contrast="none">250</span><span data-contrast="none">米，</span><span data-contrast="none">蜿蜒盘旋的形状有点像亚马逊河。水道落差</span><span data-contrast="none">3.5</span><span data-contrast="none">米，水速最高可达</span><span data-contrast="none">1.5</span><span data-contrast="none">米</span><span data-contrast="none">/</span><span data-contrast="none">秒。人们只要踏进去，就会被湍急的水流推动着高速往下冲，很多人被吓得大呼小叫，却也有种欲罢不能的兴奋感。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">还有高达</span><span data-contrast="none">27</span><span data-contrast="none">米的滑梯塔，有四个冲水滑梯可供不同年龄组的人玩耍。有比较温和的下滑方式，也有非常刺激的加速下滑方式，最高速度可达</span><span data-contrast="none">70</span><span data-contrast="none">公里</span><span data-contrast="none">/</span><span data-contrast="none">小时，适合勇敢者来挑战。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">另外，热带岛屿名副其实，种植了</span><span data-contrast="none">600</span><span data-contrast="none">多种</span><span data-contrast="none">新奇的热带</span><span data-contrast="none">植物</span><span data-contrast="none">，有</span><span data-contrast="none">食虫植物、香蕉树、咖啡灌木、丛林攀缘植物</span><span data-contrast="none">等等。还专门</span><span data-contrast="none">建有一个红鹤沼泽，生活着不同的热带鸟类。将近一公里的步道穿越了森林及红树林区，</span><span data-contrast="none">大家</span><span data-contrast="none">可以</span><span data-contrast="none">不必出国，就享受小岛度假的乐趣。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p>地址：Tropical-Islands-Allee 1, 15910 Krausnick<br />
时间：每天6:00-24:00<br />
