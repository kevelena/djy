<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>半月湾的海景海鲜餐吧</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/weddingsLobsterClambake-collage_r4_c2-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>十分霸气的婚宴海鲜大餐。（湾区海鲜餐吧，Sam’s Chowder House网站）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月02日讯】文：勤宜?大纪元</p>
<h2>Flying Fish &amp; Grill</h2>
<p>这是一家有25年历史的<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E9%A4%90%E5%90%A7.md">海鲜餐吧</a>，店家的炸鱼和薯条在美国排名第9，不一定是最健康的食品，但是能名列前茅，也是有他的道理吧！加上很好的地理位置――座落在半月弯边的水景餐吧，店内还有茂密的植物，是年轻人聚会的好场所。以海鲜食材为主的各类美食也很丰富，“提供最好的海鲜食品”是店家对客户的承诺。从海鲜开胃菜到沙拉、主食和三明治，选择丰富，25年的经验还是值得一试。<figure id="attachment_11358254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/12.31.15-5.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/12.31.15-5-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" class="size-large wp-image-11358254" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Flying Fish &amp;Grill室内的用餐环境。（湾区<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E9%A4%90%E5%90%A7.md">海鲜餐吧</a>，Flying Fish &amp; Grill谷歌地图）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11358255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/image545288-4.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/image545288-4-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391" class="size-large wp-image-11358255" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤土司佐干贝鲜虾。（湾区海鲜餐吧，Flying Fish &amp; Grill网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>Flying Fish &amp; Grill联络方式：<br />

Half Moon Bay, CA 94019<br />
电话：650-712-1125<br />

<h2>Barbara’s Fishtrap</h2>
<p>一家颇有家庭风格的海景餐吧，风景独好，人气旺。食材新鲜是其特点。店家提供各类油炸的海鲜搭配薯条，赢得不少食客的胃。这家可以外卖（外卖只收现金）。有顾客超爱他家的炸鱼和薯条，感觉这家是自己吃过的所有炸鱼薯条中的前三名。但也有食客抱怨份量不够大，味道寡淡等。不过一位资深的炸鱼和薯条的英国食客，坚信这家外酥里嫩的炸鱼是最好的，理由是炸鱼和薯条是英国的特色食品，吃过无数炸鱼和薯条的他，对此非常有发言权。究竟如何，还有待自己去品尝。<figure id="attachment_11358256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/6-2.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/6-2-600x286.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="286" class="size-large wp-image-11358256" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">食客们享用炸鱼和薯条。（<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E7%82%B8%E9%B1%BC.md">湾区炸鱼</a>，Barbara’s Fishtrap网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11358258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1-6-1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1-6-1-600x286.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="286" class="size-large wp-image-11358258" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海鲜炸物。（<a href="https://github.com/dfh2/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E7%82%B8%E9%B1%BC.md">湾区炸鱼</a>，Barbara’s Fishtrap网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>Barbara’s Fishtrap联络方式：<br />

Half Moon Bay, CA 94018<br />

网址：<a href="http://barbarasfishtrap.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">barbarasfishtrap.com</a></p>
<h2>Sam’s Chowder House</h2>
<p>没有预订就要准备好排队的海景餐馆。煎鱼、帝王蟹卷、海鲜杂烩汤、西班牙海鲜饭等都是食客们追捧的，完了之后还有美滋滋的冰激淋??给很多携家人来的顾客留下美好的回忆。餐厅外的大露台面对大海，并品尝美食，和家人朋友享受惬意的休闲时光，也是举办婚宴的好地方。预定有限，没有提前预订的话有时候，需要一个小时的等位。<figure id="attachment_11358259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/weddingsLobsterClambake-collage_r3_c2-1.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/weddingsLobsterClambake-collage_r3_c2-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-11358259" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龙虾组合餐。（湾区海鲜餐吧，Sam’s Chowder House网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11358260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/weddingsLobsterClambake-collage_r4_c2-3.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/weddingsLobsterClambake-collage_r4_c2-3-600x316.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="316" class="size-large wp-image-11358260" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">十分霸气的婚宴海鲜大餐。（湾区海鲜餐吧，Sam’s Chowder House网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>Sam’s Chowder House联络方式：<br />
地址：4210 N Cabrillo Hwy.<br />
Half Moon Bay, CA 94019<br />
电话：650-712-0245<br />
网址：<a href="http://samschowderhouse.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">samschowderhouse.com</a></p>
<h2>Moss Beach Distillery</h2>
<p>位于摩斯海滩的悬崖峭壁之上（Moss Beach），拥有辽阔海景的海鲜餐馆。也承办各种宴会、Party、会议的活动。客户对他们的食品料理和服务品质也挺满意。坐在海景露台上喝一杯（食客推荐他家的鸡尾酒），你不禁感叹：生活如此美好，好好珍惜！露台上有取暖的火炉，店家还提供毯子，以适应加州傍晚的温度。虽然有食客吐槽他家生蚝不够新鲜，不过此店在游客中知名度颇高。<figure id="attachment_11358261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/couple-sitting-by-patio-firepit-watching-sunset-8.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/couple-sitting-by-patio-firepit-watching-sunset-8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-11358261" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这里有绝美的海景与夕阳。（湾区海景餐厅，Moss Beach Distillery网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11358262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/steamed-clams-9605fcf5-7.jpg"><img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/steamed-clams-9605fcf5-7-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" class="size-large wp-image-11358262" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蒸蛤蜊。（湾区海景餐厅，Moss Beach Distillery网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>地址：140 Beach Way<br />
Moss Beach, CA 94038<br />
电话：650-728-5595<br />
网址：<a href="http://mossbeachdistillery.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">mossbeachdistillery.com</a></p>
