<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>安省警方破黑手党团伙 缴数千万元非法财物</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月18日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者周月谛报导）</span><span class="s1">安省<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A6%E5%85%8B%E5%8C%BA%E8%AD%A6%E6%96%B9.md">约克区警方</a></span><span class="s2">7</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">18</span><span class="s1">日（周四）称，已拘捕</span><span class="s2">9</span><span class="s1">名黑手党成员，并缴获总值逾</span><span class="s2">3,500</span><span class="s1">万加元的非法财物。这是警方有史以来捣毁的最大犯罪集团。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">警方从</span><span class="s2">2018</span><span class="s1">年初开始展开调查，主要针对<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%89%8B%E5%85%9A%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA%E9%9B%86%E5%9B%A2.md">黑手党犯罪集团</a></span><span class="s2">Figliomeni</span><span class="s1">。警方收集的情报显示，该犯罪集团涉嫌洗黑钱、非法赌博及放高利贷。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">大约</span><span class="s2">500</span><span class="s1">名警员参与本案的调查。他们在大多伦多地区搜查</span><span class="s2">21</span><span class="s1">套住宅、</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">间赌场（</span><span class="s2">gaming houses</span><span class="s1">）及</span><span class="s2">16</span><span class="s1">户商家。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">经过</span><span class="s2">18</span><span class="s1">个月的努力，警方已拘捕</span><span class="s2">9</span><span class="s1">个黑手党成员，其中包括黑手党头目</span><span class="s2">Angelo Figlomeni</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">警方还缴获总值逾</span><span class="s2">3,500</span><span class="s1">万加元的非法财物，如大多伦多地区的</span><span class="s2">27</span><span class="s1">栋房产（约</span><span class="s2">2,400</span><span class="s1">万元）、</span><span class="s2">23</span><span class="s1">辆豪华车（包括</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">辆法拉利）、珠宝、洋酒、手表、现金、赌场筹码。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">“这次拘捕行动意味着约克区犯罪家族的势力大幅削弱。”约克区警察总长乔里夫（</span><span class="s2">Eric Jolliffe</span><span class="s1">）说：“在过去</span><span class="s2">18</span><span class="s1">个月里，警方投入大量资源，包括受过最好训练的调查员，以结束这个家族的跨国洗钱与非法赌博活动。”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">就在召开记者会的当天，加国警方与意大利警方合作，在意大利拘捕多名嫌犯。警方的调查仍在继续。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">之前，加拿大税务局、海关（</span><span class="s2">CBSA</span><span class="s1">）、金融交易与报告分析中心、安省警方、多伦多警方、滑铁卢警方、汉密尔顿警方、皮尔区警方（</span><span class="s2">PRP</span><span class="s1">）、杜咸区警方（</span><span class="s2">DRP</span><span class="s1">）及巴里警方（</span><span class="s2">BPS</span><span class="s1">）也参与了本案的调查。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">“这个犯罪集团很暴力，影响社区里的每个人。”乔里夫说：“他们被捕后，约克区变得更安全。”</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：岳怡</p>
