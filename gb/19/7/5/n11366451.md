<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏天午餐不用蒸 来个“冷便当”：双莓拼盘</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-600x400.png" />
<h6>冷便当“双莓拼盘”。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>便当就是要加热？NO！许多食物/料理常温就能吃！<br />
加热便当才好吃？NO！日本人的便当就从来不加热。不反复加热，营养反而不易流失！<br />
<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md">冷便当</a>可以瘦身？YES！冷饭的热量比热饭低，减轻卡路里！</p>
<p>作者金希呈墙峄槭年的在职妈妈，育有两名儿子。为了先生健康而开始自制的轻食便当。她的便当以“kongkong便当”之名而为人所知，特色是忙碌的早晨也能火速完成，连色、香、味、营养都十分讲究。</p>
<p><span style="color: #339966;">“活力<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md">冷便当</a>”五大特色</span>：</p>
<p>1、打开即食免加热：利用早晨20分钟制作完成，中午免再加热，即可享用美味便当。<br />
2、跳脱传统便当菜色：捏成饭团饭卷、组合成沙拉拼盘，有如餐厅级料理，让人耳目一新。<br />
3、轻松料理少油烟：切一切、拌一拌、卷一卷即完成，极少需要开火，做菜不再满头大汗。<br />
4、原型食物、营养均衡不发胖：一主菜三配菜的设计，少淀粉、高纤高蛋白，营养不发胖。<br />
5、丰盛饱足还能当下午茶：轻食料理却不减饱足感。如果只吃主菜就饱了，副菜也可以作为下午茶点心。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>双莓奶油乳酪开放式<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%98%8E%E6%B2%BB.md">三明治</a></strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>材料：</strong></p>
<p>? 厚度 1 公分的全麦长棍面包 3 片<br />
? <a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md">草莓</a> 12 颗<br />
? <a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8E%93.md">蓝莓</a> 12 粒<br />
? 奶油乳酪 1 大匙<br />
? 糖粉少许（可省略）</p>
<p><b>作法：</b></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-1.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373311" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-1.png" alt="" width="134" b="163" /></a></p>
<p>1.将10 颗<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md">草莓</a>横切成厚度0.5公分的薄片，剩下2颗在蒂头处划V字后，纵切成两半。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-2.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373312" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-2.png" alt="" width="140" b="162" /></a></p>
<p>2.热锅中摆上长棍面包后，正反面用小火各煎1分钟。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-3.png"><img class="alignnone wp-image-11373314 size-full" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-3.png" alt="" width="136" b="177" /></a></p>
<p>3.如图所示，将1片长棍面包分成4等分，每份呈三角形。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-4.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373315" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-4.png" alt="" width="141" b="176" /></a></p>
<p>4.在长棍面包上均匀抹上奶油乳酪后，摆上草莓、<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8E%93.md">蓝莓</a>，再撒上糖粉。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>双莓<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%98%94.md">奶昔</a></strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>材料：</strong><br />
? 冷冻综合莓果（100g）1 杯<br />
? 草莓（100g）1 杯<br />
? 矿泉水（100ml）1/2 杯<br />
? 原味优格少许</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>Tip:双莓<a href="https://github.com/cbzs/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%98%94.md">奶昔</a>食材替代物</strong></span><br />
可以使用冷冻综合莓果或草莓其中一种，或是用其它莓果类（蓝莓、覆盆子等）代替。不过，总重量应为200公克。</p>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-5.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373318" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-5.png" alt="" width="134" b="161" /></a></p>
<p>1.依序在调理机中放入矿泉水→冷冻综合莓果→草莓，再搅碎。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-6.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373321" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-6.png" alt="" width="136" b="177" /></a></p>
<p>2.将①装入碗中。将原味优格装入尖嘴调味罐中。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">奶昔装饰1</span></strong></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-7.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373322" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-7.png" alt="" width="140" b="174" /></a></p>
<p>将原味优格挤成星形，再用竹签由内而外画圆，越画越大。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>奶昔装饰1</strong></span></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-8.png"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11373324" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p172-8.png" alt="" width="138" b="175" /></a></p>
<p>各处挤上圆点状的优格，再用竹签将圆点连起来，做出造型。@</p>
